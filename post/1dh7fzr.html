Real Madrid star Mario Hezonja close to NBA return, via Eurohoops

Source

Hezonja averaged 14/5/1 on 64% TS (49/45/92 shooting splits), made the All-Euroleague Second Team and was one of the key reasons why Real Madrid made the Euroleague final the past Euroleague season.

He also won the Spanish league with Real this season, as well as winning the Euroleague the season before.

The former number 5 overall draft pick is currently amidst a transfer saga in Europe, with multiple teams, including Barcelona and Euroleague champions Panathinaikos, chasing after his signature, but the possibility of an NBA return is also on the table.

The 29-year-old Croatian spent five seasons in the NBA in the past, with the Magic, the Knicks and the Blazers.
