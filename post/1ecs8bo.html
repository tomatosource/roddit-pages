Your Expectations for Damian Lillard in the 2024⁄25 NBA season.

Think Dame had a better season than some of the naysayers have said he did, his defense by the numbers was not as bad as the narrative. If you really comb through the defensive numbers, he was not awful on defense last year. His season was still an adventure with some big swings in performance, peaks and valleys, offensively it was a bit of a weird season for Dame, TS% lower than usual, but not dramatically outside his norms.

He got into 73 games which is a good number, but suffered through more leg issues, worryingly an achilles late in the season. He’s had all kinds of leg injuries the last several seasons and is 34 now, but offensively he remains an elite player, and you would imagine with a normal offseason and more ability to acclimate under Doc and Giannis that he can have a more consistent season coming up.
