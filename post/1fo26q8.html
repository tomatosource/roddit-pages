Wilt and Russell pick their five(ish)!

In this awesome Ahmad Rashad interview with Wilt Chamberlain and Bill Russell, Rashad asks the two legends to pick their top 5 (at 6:36 mark, but watch the whole thing), with the caveat that they can’t pick themselves or each other.

Wilt: Names Larry Bird unequivocally first. Then adds West, Baylor, Oscar before adding Magic/Jordan (“gimme six”). Then asks if he can have a 7th, Charles Barkley!

Russell: Magic, Jordan, West, Baylor, and Oscar for his 5, along with Olajuwon and Pettit.

Kareem doesn’t make either list! Neither did Dr. J.
