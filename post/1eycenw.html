Is it more difficult to make it undersized in the NBA than ever before?

In this current NBA it feels like it’s harder to make it in the league and ACTUALLY make a name for yourself as an undersized player than previous era. Back then we had notable short players (NBA height standards) like I.T, TY Lawson, Nate Robinson, Muggsy, Jameer Nelson and hell even Spud. Point is, to me being undersized and I’m not saying it was never an advantage in the NBA but it’s definitely more of a disadvantage NOW than previous era, with the likes of Point Forward being more prominent.
