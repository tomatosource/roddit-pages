Why are people calling Bronny’s form a slump?

Cannot understand why people are calling Bronny’s form a slump. To be a slump you have to have been coming off some sort of higher average and to date he has not reached any such average. His form is not a slump. His form is what you get when you take a 6’1 player, ranked 7th best on the 88th ranked college team in the country who averaged a paltry 4.8 ppg and you throw him in a league full of teams that would be equivalent to final 4 teams in march madness. His opponents are a significant mark up from what he faced at USC and as such you would expect nothing better than a 4ppg average and a drop off in percentages. Today for example he was the worst player, bench included, on his team, despite having the equal second most minutes. He was barely cut out for college ball, is definitely not cut out for G league and has zero business being on an NBA court as a player.
