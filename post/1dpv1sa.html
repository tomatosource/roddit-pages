The 26th pick in the 2024 draft was traded five times

The pick was initially the Clippers’.

In 2019 it was traded to OKC as part of the SGA/PG trade.

In 2024 OKC traded it to Dallas for a pick swap.

A few minutes later Dallas traded it to Washington for Daniel Gafford.

On draft night Washington traded it to New York for the 24th pick (which was originally Dallas’, too).

A few minutes later New York traded it to OKC for five seconds.

OKC drafted Dillon Jones.
