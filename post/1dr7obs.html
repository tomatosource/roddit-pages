Jimmy Butler has inexplicably decided not to pursue an extension so that Miami can realistically threaten to open up $78.5m in cap space next offseason

If you’ve listened to the Lowe Post or some of the more plugged in Heat podcasts you’ve probably heard that Jimmy’s philosophy with contracts has always been been, “pay me my money and i’ll figure the winning part out later”, every two or so years he wants to re-up for a max extension. It looked like that’s what was going to play out this offseason, however, Jimmy said “nah i’m cool don’t need an extension”. For a minute there were whispers of, “maybe Jimmy wants to leave!?” Nope, Jimmy’s camp immediately reached out to Ira Winderman and clarified that he’s happy in Miami and he’s going to play great and oh btw he loves Miami.

3 teams reportedly would give Jimmy the extension he supposedly wanted but a man that’s never been willing to risk not getting his money ASAP has decided money isn’t everything as he enters his age 35 season.
