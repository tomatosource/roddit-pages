Most Beloved Former NBA Player

I just watched another interview with Magic Johnson and once again came away impressed by his humor, stand up positions, kindness towards other players and overall likability. This triggers my thoughts on others so we’ll revered. Both Bill Russell and Bill Walton stood on high ground for years as did Larry Bird and Michael Jordan but many others not so much. Not slighting Isaiah Thomas or others I can’t recall but who was the NBA ambassador like Magic?

In a similar though less brilliant manner I love watching Shaq and Charles Barkley go after each other.. playfully, and think that it lets the world see them as more human, not just amazing athletes. And Barkley’s humor and game insights are off the charts. And while Jordan kept his act crisp, clean and professional he wasn’t ever as well liked ‘personally’ as Magic or others.

Am I missing someone else who stepped into a bigger more beloved public persona AFTER they retired?
