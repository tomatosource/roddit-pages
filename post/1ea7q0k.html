[OC] Which team USA stars are the most beloved/hated? A sentiment analysis on all reddit comments based on the five game-threads.

Convinced by an impassioned plea from Sixers fans that Embiid has been wrongfully maligned, I decided to get all the comments from every game-thread to get a better idea of the general sentiment around every team USA player from the 5 exhibition matches.

Here are the Results -

Lebron James

Comments relating to Lebron generated an average of 4.63 upvotes, on average. The average sentiment for all Lebron’s comments was largely positive, 0.44. People seem to love LeCaptain America.

Here’s a wordCloud of all comments relating to Lebron - https://imgur.com/a/F2jroyk

I’m going to recycle the link, so be a good fella and just scroll down for other players.

Devin Booker

Comments relating to Devin Booker generated an average of 4.25 upvotes, not a lot. He just hasn’t been polarizing enough so far. The average sentiment for all booker comments was mildly positive/neutral 0.02.

Sorry Book, fans just to care about you enough.

Here’s a wordCloud of all comments relating to Book - https://imgur.com/a/F2jroyk

Joel Embiid

Here comes Mr.Controversy. People either love him or hate him, by people I mean Philly fans love him and everyone else hates him. Or do they?

Comments relating to Embiid generated an average of 5.13 upvotes, on average. The average sentiment for all Embiid’s comments was largely negative, -0.24. People seem to hate Joel.

Also, word of advice kiddos. Hating on Joel works for karma generation purposes. So, if you want that sweet karma, keep on hating.

Lot’s of colourful language for Embiid = https://imgur.com/a/F2jroyk

Steph Curry

The beloved golden child! How can you hate Steph Curry?

Comments relating to Curry generated an average of 4.77 upvotes, on average. The average sentiment for all Curry’s comments was largely positive, 0.21. Like I said, how can you hate the sky-f*cker?

Here’s a wordCloud of all comments relating to Curry - https://imgur.com/a/F2jroyk

Anthony Davis

The hero! And a man that has been surprisingly beloved.

Comments relating to AD generated an average of 4.95 upvotes, on average. The average sentiment for all AD’s comments was largely positive, 0.37. People seem to love the big lug for USA.
