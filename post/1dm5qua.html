[Vecenie] 2024 NBA Draft Guide

Sam Vecenie, NBA draft guru for The Athletic, recently released his annual draft guide.

140k words, 125 pages, more than enough to give a good sense on pretty much any prospect you’re interested in. Top 100 big board, player tiers, stats and testing numbers, breakdowns and scouts.

Can follow this tweet to the PDF, which is password protected (paywall, need an Athletic sub).

Gonna list the prospects in his top 5 tiers. 28 players in total, who he views as 1st round talents. Doesn’t have a tier 1-2 in this class, sees the draft starting at tier 3.
