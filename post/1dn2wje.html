Should individual standout performances that occurred against the 2023⁄2024 Atlanta Hawks receive an asterisk due to their absurd frequency and frequent absurdity?

To put it into further context, here’s a quote I received when I posed the question to our team’s subreddit

“Statistically, this team had the 5th worst defense in NBA history. I still don’t believe the fans of this team understand that. We’re all-time bad defensively right now. Team defensive ratings have been tracked back 51 years to 1973, only 4 teams in the last 51 seasons had a worse defense than the Hawks this year. We aren’t bad defensively, we’re atrocious……”

An Outlier is defined as single data point that goes far outside the average value of a group of statistics. Performances by players against the Atlanta Hawks would fit this definition comparatively to their performances against the rest of the league. Let’s group some of these performances by the following categories.

The “I know this player is a superstar but you’re not even trying to stop them” category.

(2/3/24) Steph Curry 60 Point game

(1/26/24) Luka Doncic 73 point game

The “This player was not receiving regular minutes throughout the regular season” category

(4/3/24) Malachai Flynn 50 point game

(3/13/24) Dalano Banton 31 point game

(11/14/23) Marvin Bagley III 22⁄11 game

(12/11/23) Julian Strawther 22 point game

(1/28/24) Jordan Nwora 24 point game

(1/7/24) Caleb Houstan 25 point game

The “All-star injury replacement level player” category

(11/22/23) Mikal Bridges 45 point game

(12/18/23) Cade Cunningham 43 point game

(12/23/23) Desmond Bane 37 point game

(3/13/24) Dominic “Dominayton” Ayton 33⁄19

The “Rudy Gobert scored 25 points on perfect efficiency” category

(4/12/24) Rudy Gobert 25 points(10⁄10 FG)

And The “He’s just a regular guy, why are you making him look like Kobe?” category.

(2/12/24) Ayo Dosunmu 29 point game

(2/29/24) Cameron Johnson 29 point game

(1/22/24) Harrison Barnes 32 point game

(12/26/23) Andre Drummond 24⁄25 game

(11/30/23) Jeremy Sochan 33 point game (12⁄14 FG)

(12/20/23) Jabari Smith Jr. 34 point game

Look at the names on this list. Look at the length of this list. It’s like the NBA’s own miniature equivalent of the MLB steroid era, but isolated to performances against a single team for a single year. Some performances are more unlikely than others, but for the sake of fairness every single one of these performances, and probably several others that I missed(likely subconsciously for the sake of my mental health) should have an asterisk stating that they occurred against the 2023-24 Atlanta Hawks. This is in order to contextualize them in hopes that they aren’t accidentally misconstrued at a later date in history as having happened against a team that believed in the concept of defense, for statistical integrity.
