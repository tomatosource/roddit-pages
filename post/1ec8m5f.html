Collin Sexton averaged 19 PPG/5 AST in 26.6 minutes per game in 2023⁄24 on 49⁄39% shooting and 61 TS%. Is he being underrated?

Sexton is still only 25 years old and has really started coming into his own, with a career high assist rate last season by a huge margin and best AST/TO ratio, elite finishing at the rim for a guard, increasing his volume from 3 which is something many have been wanting to see him do while remaining a high level 3 point shooter. Has a good floater too.

He never will be a natural PG and will turn the ball over a little too much and the defense is obviously not a plus, although that is exacerbated by he being forced to defend the SG spot next to a rookie PG. The season before last it was next to Conley. Those two minuses in his game make him a trickier fit on several teams, but he’s also at this stage a top 50 offensive player in the league. Sexton over the last two seasons has been a top 50 offensive player. But he doesn’t get that level of credit.
