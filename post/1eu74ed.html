Why did the 76ers trade Jrue Holiday for the 2013 Sixth Overall Pick and a 2014 1st Round Pick (Top 5 Protected)?

Holiday was coming off a 22 year old All-Star season. A sixth overall pick is a… crapshoot at best and likewise with a top 5 protected pick. They ended up drafting Nerlens Noel with the 2013 sixth overall and the tenth pick in 2014, which they traded down for the 12th pick which was Dario Saric. Getting a 22 year old All Star is kind of like best case scenario for that sort of pick. Holiday would go on to be a huge part of two NBA Championship teams and probably could have been a building block if they kept him around. Was that just the 76ers trying to be the smartest guy in the room?
