Video analysis of NBA top 75 Dolph Schayes - from JJ Redick’s comment on Dolph Schayes “I would kick his ass and be top 75”

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7DnKQPpDlrY&

So I started a new video series that takes a close look at NBA history and tries to add context so we can appreciate the value of historical players skill and abilities and not make the same mistakes JJ Redick made, casually writing them off without context. Currently I’m working on episode 3 which will be about Willis Reed but this is the second episode of the series. The structure of the game was different back then and few people, recent NBA players included, seem to realize how different it was. I don’t see a lot of videos or people really diving into that topic and I feel it needs to be clarified or players in the past often get mislabeled as unskilled or inefficient when actually a lot of them were literally the opposite given what they were allowed to do - the rules were just so drastically different our expectations are out of touch. Hopefully this, and future videos will help shape an appreciation for past NBA legends such as Dolph Schayes.
