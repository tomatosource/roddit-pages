With their opening game last night, the PBA (second oldest league after the NBA) officially welcomes the 4 pt shot.

As the title says, the PBA officially implements the four point shot into the game of professional basketball, at least in the Philippines.

The 4 pt shot is a bit farther than the 3pt line and I wish I could say it received a mixed reaction from Filipino fans, but, alas, it has been clowned ever since it was first considered.

If I’m not mistaken, 4 4pt shots were made in last night’s game between Meralco Bolts and Magnolia Hot Shots, with former Warriors Glen Robinson III heaving a 4 pt prayer to try and win the game for the Hot Shots.
