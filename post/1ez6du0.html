Which is the most important box score stat?

(English isn’t my first language so I apologize if I end up making mistakes)

Of all the traditional Box Score stats (PTS, REB, AST, BLK, STL and FG%), which one would you say is the most important in terms of winning or team impact?

Of course, every aspect of a basketball player is equally important at the end of the day, but still, me personally if I have to choose, I’m going for assists. Simply because I believe playmaking/passing to be the most fundamental aspect in team basketball. At the end of the day, EVERY team needs to have good ball movement in order to win and a player’s individual playmaking ability adds a lot to that. There’s a reason for why every player’s favourite teammate is usually a “pass first” kind of player.
