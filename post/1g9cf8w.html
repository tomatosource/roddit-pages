NBA Retired and Active Jersey Numbers after Finalized Rosters (2024-25 Opening Night)

I made a spreadsheet of all retired and active jersey numbers of each NBA team after the roster finalizations for opening night 2024.

Some interesting notes:

  • Only 1 team has the jersey #1 available or unretired: Chicago Bulls
  • Only 1 team has the jersey #5 available or unretired: Washington Wizards
  • Most worn jersey number currently: #5 worn by 26 teams
  • Only 2 active players using jersey #6 after it was retired: DeAndre Jordan of Denver and Jaylin Williams of OKC
  • Players that do not share the same numbers with anyone in the league: Marcus Smart (36), John Konchar (46), Sandro Mamukelashvili (54), Jack McVeigh (58), Pat Spencer (61), Taj Gibson (67), Taze Moore (76), and Armel Traore (94)
  • The most retired jersey number in the league (aside from #6): #32 which is retired by 13 teams, followed by #33 retired by 9 teams, and #24 retired by 8 teams
