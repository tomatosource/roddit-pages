Highest payed NBA players - salary vs franchise value - 1984 to 2024

 

A lot has already been made of Jason Tatum’s new 314 million 5-year extension. It does seem like a gigantic amount, which it is. Even though it’s the biggest NBA contract to have been signed to date, is he really the highest paid player of all time when contrasting a player’s yearly salary vs the estimated value of the franchise they play/played for? I’m not sure if this really qualifies as finding the “highest paid player” of the last 40 years, but it’s an interesting thought experiment.
