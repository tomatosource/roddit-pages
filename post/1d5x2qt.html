What are the best pettynyms in the league?

“Pettynym” is an arbitrary term I came up with meaning, “a derisive nickname, usually a mean pun on the person’s name.”

I genuinely love these things. Sometimes they’re really clever puns, which is great. Sometimes they’re horrible, ham-fisted things which only go to show how much the writer has no regard for that person, which is also great.

The most famous one is probably Russell Westbrick (honestly kinda clever), but there’s also all-timers like LeBum (not clever at all but oh-so-petty), Anthony Day-to-Davis (clever!), Pandemic P or Wayoff P (vs. Playoff P).
