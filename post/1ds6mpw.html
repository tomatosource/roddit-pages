[Fischer] If George flees the Clippers, Harden is still expected to find his own three year agreement as an avenue to get under the second apron to open access to the $5.2 million tax-payer MLE

James Harden is willing to take one less year on his deal and less than $30 million annually to allow the Clippers to avoid the second apron and put winning pieces around himself and Kawhi Leonard

It appears that Harden is once again willing to take a pay cut to help his team fill out their roster regardless of if PG goes or not, marking the fourth time in his career that he’s almost consecutively either turned down a big contract for a better basketball situation or taken a paycut to help the team.

In 2020, Harden turned down a $103 million 2 year extension, and everyone said it was to take the bag in Brooklyn.

In 2021, Brooklyn offered him a max extension and he turned it down because he was unsure of the long-term viability of that team. Everyone again said it was to get a bigger bag in Philly

In 2022, he took a pay cut to help Philly round out their roster instead of forcing them to give him a bigger contract.

Now in 2024, he’s helping the Clippers stay under the second apron to open up the MLE instead of bending them over a barrel like he could.

he’s opening their negotiations saying he’s willing to take a contract worth less than $90m over 3 years. whether or not he’d be able to get more else where is irrelevant - that is objectively a sacrifice for the clippers.
