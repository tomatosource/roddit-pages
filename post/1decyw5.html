Slovenian Prime Minister Robert Golob has flown into Texas to watch game 3 of the 2024 Finals

Slovenian Prime Minister Robert Golob flew to Texas on Wednesday for Game 3, too. On Instagram, the politician wrote “All my life I dreamed of watching the NBA Finals and today I’m going to cheer for Luka in Dallas.”

Cool to see Luka and the Mavs receive this level of support from his home country. Going to be a lot of Slovenians in Texas this week. Hopefully it’s a great game 3 for the audience.
