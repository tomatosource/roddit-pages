Do you think Point Guards will continue to be taller?

Pre 2000s and 2000s. We saw a lot of Under 6’3 Point Guards in the league who are quality level players. (Magic being 6’9” was a revelation Back then)

Nowadays, Luka is 6’7-6’8. SGA is around 6’6.

And you have 6’6-6’8 PG players like Lamelo, Lonzo, Amen, Goddey, Cade, Black, Castle.

Heck, Ben Simmons is still in the league. And he is a 6’10” PG.

There are still good to great Under 6’5 PGs, but it seems like 6’7-6’8 will be the future sweet height spot for a quality PG in the future.

There will always be under 6’5 PG who are good (Nolan Traore will be drafted next year, and seems legit). But I think a decade or two decades from now.

6’7 PG will be a norm, and not a revelation. Even a 6’9 PG will not be an anomaly like 80s people thought of Magic.
