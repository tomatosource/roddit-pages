17 year old and projected 2025 top 10 pick Khaman Maluach finishes with 7 points, 3 rebounds, and 1 block vs the US before fouling out in 12 minutes. He already is one of the youngest players ever to play in a major international competition.

Khaman Maluach is super raw, but he was probably better than expected today. He looked way stronger than you would think for most 17 year olds of his build, and he had some good moments. He obviously had major foul trouble in only 12 mins, but that’s to be expected for a young player playing against elite competition.

The 2025 draft is pretty strong. As a prospect, Maluach is at least on the same tier, if not higher, than Alex Sarr, who was a consensus top 2 prospect in the 2024 draft. Yet Maluach is projected only around 6-10 right now for the 2025 draft.

He already played in the FIBA World Cup last year as a 16 year old and will be a 17 year old Olympian this year.

There’s only a very small list of players who played in major international competitions as a teenager (includes Ricky Rubio, Karl Anthony Towns, Jamal Murray, also VJ Edgecombe in the same 2025 class), but those players, besides Rubio, didn’t do it at the Olympics.

Maluach will have a chance to do so at the Olympics in a week before teaming up with Cooper Flagg at Duke in the upcoming college season.
