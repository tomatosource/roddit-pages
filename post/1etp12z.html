What’s the most underrated single season in NBA history?

Bonus points for finding a season that’s not just forgotten, but was overlooked in awards voting that year.

My pick:

08-09 Chris Paul: 22.8 points, 11.0 assists, 5.5 rebounds, 2.8 steals on just 3.0 turnovers, at 59.9 TS% with good enough defence he was 6th in DPOY voting.

He willed the N.O. Hornets to 49 wins and the 7th seed with an injury-plagued starting lineup of David West, Rasul Butler, Tyson Chandler and an old Peja.

By Win Shares it would rank the 16th best MVP season of all time, ahead of every MVP season by anyone not named Kareem, Wilt, Oscar, Jordan, Lebron or Shaq — but he finished 5th in voting that year (behind Bron, Kobe, Wade and Dwight, in that order) and was only 2nd-team All-NBA (behind Wade and Kobe at guard).

And yes, the Hornets lost the first round to a good Nuggets team.

(If it wasn’t for this season, I’d pick Wade from the same year.)
