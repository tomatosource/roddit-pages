[Pompey] Philadelphia 76ers expected to re-sign Tyrese Maxey to five-year, $205 million deal this offseason.

The team is also expected to re-sign Maxey, an All-Star point guard, to a five-year, $205 million deal.

Important to note that the Sixers have Maxey’s bird rights, which means they can go over the cap to re-sign him. So, that means they can do whatever free agency moves they want first, like signing another max contract, and once it is all used up they can still extend Maxey because of these rights. Guessing he’ll probably be the final free agency signing for them with this in mind.
