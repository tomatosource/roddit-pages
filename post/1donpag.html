Jrue Holiday’s trade to the Celtics - 2 firsts. Pascal Siakam’s trade to the Pacers - 3 firsts. Mikal Bridges to the Knicks - 5 firsts, 1 second & a swap

For those who want an outline of the overall packages:

Pascal Siakam

  • 2024 Pacers 1st (19th in this draft)
  • 2024 worst of OKC’s million 1sts (29th in this draft; traded to the Jazz for Ochai Agbaji, Kelly Olynyk)
  • 2026 Pacers 1st (Top 4 protected)
  • Bruce Brown
  • Jordan Nwora
  • Kira Lewis Jr. (Traded in the above Ochai/Olynyk move)

Jrue Holiday

  • Warriors 2024 1st (14th pick in this draft)
  • 2029 Celtics 1st (Unprotected)
  • Malcolm Brogdon
  • Robert Williams

Mikal Bridges

  • 2025 Knicks 1st (Unprotected)
  • 2027 Knicks 1st (Unprotected)
  • 2029 Knicks 1st (Unprotected)
  • 2025 Knicks 1st (Unprotected)
  • Right to swap picks in 2028 (Unprotected)
  • 2025 Bucks 1st
  • 2026 Knicks 2nd
  • Bojan Bogdanovic

Mikal got more draft capital than the Jrue/Siakam trades combined.

He has never made an All-Star game like both of them has, and his only NBA award was All-Defensive 1st Team back in 2022.
