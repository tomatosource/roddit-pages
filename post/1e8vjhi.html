Serbia defeated Japan 119-100 in a friendly match ahead of the Paris Olympics.

Box Score. Serbia in full squad for the first time this summer. Jokić had 18 points, 8 rebounds, and 7 assists in 28 minutes, Bogdanović scored 15 points, and Micić added 12 (NBA players). Jović’s injury doesn’t seem to be something that will prevent him from competing in the games, although the status of the injury has been kept secret to avoid media leaks. A great shooting night for Japan with 20 made three-pointers. Hachimura led all scorers with 29 points.

Tomorrow at 20:00 (8 PM) CET, Serbia plays Greece in their final preparatory match. Jokić vs. Giannis, and both coaches will announce the final 12-player roster after the game.
