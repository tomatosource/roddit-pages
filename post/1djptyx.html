Mazzulla on his torn meniscus:“I’m thinking about maybe getting hurt every all-star break”

From his interview on Pardon My Take (starts at 1:08:00)

Full extremely psychotic quote:

Listen, that’s what happens when you lose games in the regular season. After we lost to Atlanta at home, I went out, went on the mats, punished myself and just pounded my body until it couldn’t take anymore, and ended up just tearing my knee

It ended up being a great experience for me. I had to do 6 hours of treatment in order to coach the next game without too much of a limp because I could not walk.

But I tell you what, it was one of the best things that could happen to me for the rest of the season because it put me in this fight or flight mentality to where I could just not relax. I had to constantly train to keep it pain free. Had to constantly get physical therapy, and my physical therapist has been great.

It was awesome. I’m thinking about maybe getting hurt every all-star break

He actually credits the injury for making him more focused because he couldn’t pace around as much, and that he would do intensive jiujitsu training after every loss. He adds

The injury probably did save my life because I was getting carried away…I do miss the mat though
