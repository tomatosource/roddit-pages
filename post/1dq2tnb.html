Every single 2024 second round pick has been traded at least once

With the Orlando Magic trading the 47th to the New Orleans Pelicans to pick Antonio Reeves, every single second round pick this year has been traded at least once. An unprecedented amount of wheeling and dealing from NBA GM’s, before and during this draft.

Source: https://x.com/nateduncannba/status/1806440095110824335?s=46&t=unF-9oFydqZI2LHjKTbo_g

Nate Duncan: “[Orlando 47th pick] TRADED TO NEW ORLEANS per @ShamsCharania. So, NO second rounders will have not been traded before the pick is used.”

Only 28 picks instead of 30 this season due to the Suns and 76ers forfeiting their picks for free agent tampering.
