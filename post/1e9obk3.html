[Post Game Thread] Serbia Defeats Greece 94-72 in Final Exhibition Game Before the Paris Olympics

Box Score. Serbia in full squad again, for the second time this summer. As for the NBA players, Jokić had 16 points, 8 rebounds, and 6 assists in 23 and a half minutes. Bogdanović scored 19 points and went 5⁄5 from three-point range. Jović played again, showing that his injury is behind him - he scored 10 points in 11 minutes. Micić had 4 points and 2 assists.

For Greece, Giannis Antetokounmpo had 17 points, 6 rebounds, and 4 assists in 25 minutes.

Serbia recorded an impressive 30 team assists, while Greece was weakened by the absence of Calathes and Sloukas (their second-best player), who chose not to play for the NT this summer, feeling it wouldn’t be fair to take a spot from the players who secured Olympic qualification earlier.

Both coaches will announce the final 12-player roster either later today or tomorrow.
