The San Antonio Spurs haven’t won more than 34 games in any of the last 5 seasons and they still have by far the highest win percentage of any NBA team since 2000

The top 5 teams by win % since 2000 are the Spurs, Mavericks, Celtics, Heat and Lakers.

Spurs: 63.5%

Mavericks: 59.1%

Celtics: 56.7%

Heat: 55.8%

Lakers: 55.1%

It just shows how dominant the franchise has been despite missing the Playoffs in the last 5 years.

They won 60+ games 6 times and 55+ games 14 times.

Source: https://www.statmuse.com/nba/ask/team-with-the-best-winning-percentage-since-2000
