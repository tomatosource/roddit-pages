The best two-way seasons ever? The only players in NBA history to ever make All Defense while also averaging 30ppg+: Michael Jordan (7x), Kobe Bryant (3x), Jerry West (1x), Kareem Abdul Jabbar (1x), Dwyane Wade (1x)

These are the only times in NBA history where a player averaged 30ppg and was named to an All Defense team in the same year

Jerry West - 1970

Kareem Abdul Jabbar - 1971

Michael Jordan - 1988, 1989, 1990, 1991, 1992, 1993, 1996

Kobe Bryant - 2003, 2006, 2007

Dwyane Wade - 2009

Surprisingly Giannis has NOT accomplished this feat. Giannis has made All Defense 5x and has averaged 30ppg twice, but never in the same season has he done both. Similarly Joel Embiid has done both three times but never in the same season.
