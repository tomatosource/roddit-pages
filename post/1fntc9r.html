How well do you all expect Kelly Oubre and Caleb Martin to hold the fort as the starting forwards for the Philadelphia 76ers?

A question I’ve been meaning the ask the community for a bit, I still do believe in terms of fit and just the blend of the roster+coaching, this is rivaling the Butler Sixers for the best team Embiid has had. That team had more top end talent on paper but this team is deeper and fits Embiid like a glove theoretically.

The Sixers signed Martin to a 4/\(35M deal in the offseason to be their starter at the 4. Oubre re-signed on a 2/\)16M deal with a PO. I think Oubre was a fantastic locker room presence last year and he started out the season hot. He had a great start to his season last year and played a more focused brand of basketball, but his final numbers were essentially what they always are at the end of the season. He had a couple of hot games in April which pushed his shooting from 3 to 31%, he was almost sub-30% from 3 on the season. He got hit by a car and missed a month. He actually played OK after returning, but when Embiid got hurt and Maxey was banged up, he took on more load offensively. That’s when his efficiency went down the tubes.

He was good in the playoffs which leads you to believe that he will elevate playing with Embiid but he’s consistently been a low-30s 3 point shooter, he’s still a pretty substandard rebounder and passer, and his all around defense is still only at best average, and didn’t grade out too well last season.

Caleb Martin has some playmaking in him and can handle in transition, he will catch and rip and take it to the cup or pull up. He’s a good finisher, knows how to move off ball. He’s an active and athletic defender and a disruptor on that end. But he certainly isn’t a lockdown three point shooter or pull up shooter and often goes through some bad cold spells. He had the 40%+ 3 point season a few seasons ago but has been mid-30s the last two seasons. He’s a fine spot up shooter but not an elite one. Only OK.

Wanna know what you all think about these two as the Sixers forward duo and how well you think they hold it down for Philly around their Big 3.
