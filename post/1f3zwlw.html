In 2016-17, LeBron James finished 5th in voting for Defensive Player of the Year and 19th in voting for the All-Defensive Team.

Players receiving more votes for DPOY:

  • Draymond Green
  • Rudy Gobert
  • Kawhi Leonard
  • Robert Covington

Players receiving more votes for the All-Defensive Teams:

  • Draymond Green
  • Rudy Gobert
  • Kawhi Leonard
  • Chris Paul
  • Patrick Beverley
  • Tony Allen
  • Danny Green
  • Anthony Davis
  • Andre Roberson
  • Giannis Antetokounmpo
  • Avery Bradley
  • Klay Thompson
  • John Wall
  • DeAndre Jordan
  • Paul Millsap
  • Hassan Whiteside
  • Marcus Smart
  • Jimmy Butler

Robert Covington received more votes than LeBron for DPOY, but fewer votes than LeBron for the All-Defensive Team.
