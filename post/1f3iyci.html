Going into the 2024⁄25 NBA season, which team do you think is better on paper: Brooklyn Nets or Toronto Raptors

I think these two teams on paper are quite comparable currently. Factoring in internal growth, factoring in depth, factoring in coaching…hard to really choose one or the other. The Nets are largely predicted to be worse than the Raptors and among the very worst of the league, and while they may be eventually, and they’re surely not great as it relates to the league as a whole as it stands now, don’t see much of a gap between these two teams.

Raptors have the top end talent edge; the Nets have the edge on depth, experience, and familiarity with one another. Fernandez is highly respected as a coach in NBA circles, many believe this is a long time coming for him, but is a rookie as a HC. Rajakovic had his bumps as a rookie last year but things are a bit less unsettled going into this season which should benefit him.

Between the Nets and Raptors, who do you all think is the superior team going into the year?
