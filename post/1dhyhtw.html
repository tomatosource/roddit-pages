What is Johnathan Kuminga’s potenial?

21 year old Johnathan Kuminga had a career year for the Warriors posting 16 points on 52% from the field.

The 6’8 forward is an electrifying athlete, as shown by the thunderous dunks he threw down throughout the 23-24 season. He has a combination of agility, body control, vertical, and speed that reminds one of the great athletes in the NBA today like a young LeBron or Anthony Edwards. Some players seem to have an ability to “glide” in the air, and Kuminga is among them. Kuminga also has a long stride to the basket, and ability to finish through defenders that can remind one at times of a smaller Giannis. His combination of all of these physical attributes makes him one of the most naturally gifted players in the NBA in a very long time.

Kuminga has soft touch at the rim, and uses his combination of speed, size and athleticism to often beat his defender, if not go right through them. He’s a big time lob threat and good for some putbacks, though his rebounding is quite lacking as a whole, averaging not even a paltry 5 a game.

Kuminga showcase increased ability to cut off the ball, and would often get layups or dunks as a result. However, Kuminga’s fit with the Warriors still remains questionable. Kuminga has a tendency to go into “tunnel vision” mode, and often stalls the ball movement of the Warriors offense. It has mostly seems an issue of how to maximize the team the way Curry likes to play and the way Kuminga likes to play at the same time.

Kuminga is not a great outside shooter, but he he has shown a great ability to hit mid range shots, and jumpers in the painted area, where he displays a smooth arsenal of stepbacks.

At his best, Kuminga already looks like an all-star level player. He had a 5 game stretch averaging 26 on 65% from the field. and several other comparable stretches, but so far has struggled a bit with his consistency.

Keep in mind for his stats he only played 27 minutes a game. It has been a struggle to perhaps find the role Kuminga wants on the Warriors this far as well.
