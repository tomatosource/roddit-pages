  4. 2024 Boston Celtics: +7.9

  7. 1997 Chicago Bulls: +7.7
  8. 1998 Utah Jazz: +7.6

  10. 1996 Chicago Bulls: +7.5

Source (Thinking Basketball historical stat board)
