Sasha Vezenkov set to be released by the Raptors and return to Greek giants Olympiacos, per multiple reliable European sources

Source

First reported by Greek’s Gazzetta, the Raptors have released Sasha Vezenkov, which is expected to be made official soon, and the former Euroleague MVP has already agreed on a multi-year deal with his former club Olympiacos.

Vezenkov spent the past season averaging 5/2/1 on 57% TS on the Kings, before being traded to the Raptors for Jalen McDaniels this offseason.

Vezenkov had numerous offers from Europe, including from Olympiacos’ arch rivals Panathinaikos, but he decided to return to the club from Piraeus, on a reportedly four-year €16.000.000 deal.
