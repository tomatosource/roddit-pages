Luka averaged 28.9 points in this year’s playoffs, but didn’t score 40 a single time.

Just a curious stat. Normally when players average that much and play 15+ games, they hit the 40 mark at least once. Luka playoff-high this year was 36.

I searched for players who averaged within one point of Luka and played at least 15 games in the playoffs: Trae Young 2021, Curry 2019, Durant 2018, Harden 2018, Durant 2017, Curry 2017, Durant 2016, Curry 2015, Durant 2014, Durant 2012, Durant 2011, Kobe 2010, Amare 2005, Shaq 2001 (I didn’t go back further). From all these postseasons, only Durant in 2012 and 2017 didn’t hit 40 points, but in 2017 Durant scored 39 once and 38 twice, and in 2012 he scored 36 twice (Luka did it once).

Lebron had 11 postseasons with at least 15 games and didn’t fit the criteria once lol. But I checked and out of the 11, he scored at least 40 in 9 of them, exceptions are 2011 and 2013. Both of these years he averaged at least 3 full points under Luka though.

In his other postseasons Luka hit 40 twice in 2020, three times in 2021 (both of these playing only the first round) and three times in 2022.

I guess this means Luka was pretty consistent scoring at least in terms of raw totals, since he kept the average that high.
