[Podcast P with Paul George] Tyrese Maxey talks on waiting to sign his rookie extension with the Sixers: “It was hard. I put in a lot of work … You want to reap the benefits. You feel like you failed yourself, because of all the work you put in” [Full Quote Below]

“It was hard. Way hard. I put in a lot of work. When the work shows, and the results come, and you perform at the highest level. As far as stepping into different roles, doing what they ask you to do. You start, you get hurt, you come off the bench. You go back to starting. Play 1 play 2, do all those different things. You want to reap the benefits of course. When the opportunity presents itself, it’s hard. You see your peers get paid, reap the benefits they deserve . You feel like you failed yourself, because of all the work you put in. But I understood where they were coming from. It worked out in the long run. For myself and the team”
