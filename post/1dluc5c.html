After nine consecutive seasons featuring a player with a 300-turnover season, Doncic’s league-most 282 is the 2nd lowest in a full season in NBA history.

The only full season with a lower top turnover tally was Westbrook’s 274 as a rookie in 2008-09. Other lower tallies include John Wall’s 255 in the shortened 2011-12 season and Abdur-Rahim’s 186 turnovers in the even-shorter 1998-99 season. Arguably worth noting that Russ, Wall, and Shareef each played every game of their season, while Luka played 70 games this year.
