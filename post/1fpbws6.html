Your Ideal Uniform Set for Your Team

With the recent unveiling of the new City uniforms being met with a lukewarm reaction (at best), and a constant criticism being that NBA teams lack a consistent identity - I wanted to ask you all, the fans this: What is your ideal logo and uniform set from your teams’ history that you feel best captures its history or identity? Let’s assume Nike lets you call the shots, with the only rule being the home and road uniforms must be a matching set. The alternative and city jerseys can be from whenever.

For me, as a Rockets fan - I would choose the home & away uniforms from 1975-1995. The ketchup & mustard is iconic to Houston fans, being worn by legends such as Rudy T, Calvin, Moses, Hakeem & Clyde. For our alternate/statement jersey, I would keep the black ‘Space City’ ones we have now, worn by Harden who led us to our most successful season since ‘95. And lastly, for our “City” jersey, I’d bring back the navy pinstripes and cartoon Rockets, complete with the wild court we had in the Summit/Compaq center.

Curious to see what other fans say.
