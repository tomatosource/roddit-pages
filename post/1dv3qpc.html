Nigel Hayes-Davis, All-Euroleague First Team selection and the only player to score 50 PTS in an Euroleague game, signs a three-year contract extension with Turkish club Fenerbahce, despite considerable interest from the Suns to sign him

Source

Hayes-Davis averaged 15/5/2 on 62% TS (48/39/82 shooting splits) during the past Euroleague regular season and made the Final Four with the Turkish powerhouse.

He also was the first and only player to score 50 points in an Euroleague game, doing so against Alba Berlin on 83% TS (9⁄16 from three). Euroleague star and former NBA player Nikola Mirotic stated that it’s the equivalent of scoring 80 in the NBA.

Hayes-Davis drew considerable interest from multiple NBA teams, with the Suns being most adamant to get the 29-year-old sharpshooter back to the NBA, but ultimatelly it broke down because he doesn’t have an NBA opt out clause in his contract.

The former Wisconsin Badger has become one of the top-paid players in Europe with this new contract.
