Anthony Edwards after MN lost in the WCF: “We underestimated em’. That’s honestly what happened. That’s why we lost…I’m happy for Kyrie. Happy for Luka too. I’ll be back and better. You take your losses how you take your wins. Chin up, man. Let’s move on”. He finds Luka after the game “Go win it”.

More quotes after the loss (Episode 10 of Starting 5):

Ant admitting “We thought we were going to sweep them. As soon as we won Game 7, we like, ‘Yeah these boys cooked.’ We did underestimate ‘em. That’s just honestly what happened. That’s why we lost.”

While visibly frustrated but hopeful on the drive home: “Damn bro. I’m happy for them though. I ain’t gonna lie, I’m happy for Kyrie. I’m happy that he gets a chance to go back to the Finals without Bron, to show people that he don’t need him. I’m mad I ain’t get to chop it up with him after the game, but it’s all good. I’m happy for Luka too. I will be back. I got a lot of years. I’ll be back and better, man. Told my teammates, ‘Pick y’all heads up. Take your losses how you take your wins. Chin up, man. Let’s move on.’ “

“Feel like after you lose a series, [people] sit and mope around. You had every chance to prove to win the game. I mean, you didn’t win. Fuck it. I just think about it like, Shit we’ll be back. It’s about preparation now.”

“The preparation now for me is just getting in better shape. I think that’s the number one thing. Cause I don’t eat the best, you know? My taste buds haven’t turned the corner. People say your taste bud changes over the years. I haven’t got there yet.” *montage of Ant eating chicken wings and sitting at a restaurant asking ‘Where the biscuits at?’ *

Ant was very open throughout the series, admitting when he was overconfident and in general, openly showing his flaws.
