A Compiled List of All NBA Team Needs

Hello everybody! I have made a list of all NBA team needs based on how they statistically performed throughout the season. This list has information such as: team rosters, their players’ free agency status, their draft picks (and who I would draft), a list of NBA Free Agents and their stats, each team’s Cap Space, and more.

I created this as a fun tool to create discussion and to look at what state each team is in. For example: every player on the 76ers is entering Free Agency minus Embiid and Paul Reed (Maxey is a Restricted FA). Do you think the players I would draft or pick as free agents are fair? Who would you pick? I had a ton of fun making this Google Excel Sheet, and hope this community finds it useful.
