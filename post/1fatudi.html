Bob McAdoo finished 2nd in MVP voting for 1975-76 season but didn’t make an All-NBA that year

Just found this to be really bizarre so wanted to share. McAdoo had an incredible three season run from 1974-1976 finishing 2nd, 1st and 2nd in MVP voting respectively but across those three seasons he ended up All NBA 2nd team, 1st team and none at all (there were only 2 All-NBA teams back then). His stats are really impressive across those seasons averaging 32/14/3/1/3 on 51% FG and 79% FT shooting. League average team scoring per game was ~104 back then too so converting to today’s scoring averages would be ~35.3 PPG. He led the league in scoring in each of those seasons.

His situation is similar to Embiid’s 2020-22 seasons where finishing 2nd in MVP behind another center results in you not being on 1st team All-NBA. The exception is the 1975-76 season though, where Dave Cowens was voted 2nd team All-NBA center somehow despite finishing 3rd in the MVP voting.
