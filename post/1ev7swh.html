Who has the strongest NBA Coaching tree?

I have seen a lot of talk about Coaching trees in the NFL with Bill Belichick leaving the Patriots, and it’s got me very interested in what exists around the coaching trees of the NBA.

It seems if you trace back far enough, pretty much any good NBA coach falls into The Wooden Tree and the Naismith Tree. But I’m asking more recently, which NBA coach has the best Tree associated with them?

I think the volume vs successfulness of coaches is also very interesting. As you can see here, Andy Reed (chiefs HC) and Joe Grudden (Raiders HC) both come from the Mike Holmgren tree. Reed has produced more head coaches (8) than Grudden’s 3, but Gruddens is ostensibly more noteworthy ( McVay, Shanahan Parcells).

Pop is the coastal redwood of NBA coaches, with nearly every coach stemming from the limbs of his tree. Proof. If you exclude him and look within the branches, or outside of his reach, which NBA coach has the best tree?
