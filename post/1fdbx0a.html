This does not mean LeBron is the GOAT. It means he is a statistical anomaly, similar to Wilt. Yes, he has two more rings than Wilt so I understand why people would have him as their GOAT but Michael Jordan was also a statistical anomaly and has two more rings than LeBron so it is what it is. I think the debate is still between Kobe and LeBron. Yes, this metric shows LeBron as head and shoulders above Kobe but basketball is much more than points, rebounds, assists and Kobe has one more ring than LeBron. So, I think there is still a debate there.
