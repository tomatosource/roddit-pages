LeBron James and Bronny James officially overtake Kobe Bryant and Joe Bryant to become the father-son duo with the most combined points in NBA history

Kobe Bryant (33,643 points) and his father Joe Bryant (5,252 points) was, until today, the father-son duo with the most combined points (38,895 points).

With the drafting of Bronny James, LeBron James (40,474 points) and his son Bronny James (0 points) become the father-son duo with the most combined points (40,474 points).
