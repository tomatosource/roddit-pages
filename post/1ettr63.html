Kevin Durant and DeAndre Jordan have the same amount of All NBA 1st Team selections in the past 10 seasons.

If you’re looking at the numbers from the last 10 NBA seasons (2015-2024), here are some of their highlights:

Kevin Durant:

  • 2x NBA champion (2017, 2018)

  • 9x NBA All-Star (2015-2019, 2021-2024)

  • 1x All-NBA First Team (2018)

  • 5× All-NBA Second Team (2016, 2017, 2019, 2022, 2024)

DeAndre Jordan:

  • 1x NBA champion (2023)

  • 1x NBA All-Star (2017)

  • 1x All-NBA First Team (2016)

  • 2× All-NBA Second Team (2015, 2017)

It’s more of a surprise that KD has just 1 All NBA selection in the past 10 seasons (despite being a Top 5 player in that time frame), than it is seeing Jordan with a selection himself.
