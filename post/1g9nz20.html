For the past 40 years, nearly every NBA champ had one thing in common. A look at if your team has a realistic shot. (24⁄25 Edition)

Last season, I made a post looking at which teams had a real shot at the title based on this metric: over the past 40 years, nearly every NBA championship team has had at least one player who was a 1st-Team All-NBA selection before their title year. And once again, last season held true — The Celtics and Mavericks made the Finals.

(Please take a look at last years post if you want more details about this metric. I’m excluding players like Chris Paul who are so far outside of their prime window that they don’t really qualify.)

So, who checks that box this year?

Teams with at least one player previously selected 1st team All-NBA (in no particular order):

  1. 76ers
  2. Warriors
  3. Clippers
  4. Lakers
  5. Suns
  6. Bucks
  7. Celtics
  8. Nuggets
  9. Thunder (OKC)
  10. Mavericks

We can break it down a little more with some additional (but not quite as reliable) details.

Roster Age Weighted by Minutes Played:

The average age of championship teams, weighted for minutes played, is 28.234 years (Boston was 28.6 last season). Here are the teams that fall in the general range (:

  1. 76ers
  2. Celtics
  3. Mavericks
  4. Lakers (It’s surprising for obvious reasons)
  5. Nuggets

Teams with 2+ 1st Team All-NBA Stars:

Teams with two or more 1st team players win almost 50% of titles in the past 40 years. This is trend that is breaking in the past decade, so not nearly as reliable but still worth looking at.

  • Lakers (Bron+AD)
  • 76ers (Embiid+PG)

Teams with 2+ 1st Team players, but who’s rosters are outside of the age window:

  • Bucks (Giannis+Dame)
  • Suns (KD+Book)
  • Clips (Kawhi+Harden)

Conclusion:

Anything can happen (shoutout to my 2004 Pistons), but it’s very unlikely any team other than the ones listed above will win it all this season. This means teams that seem like contenders are probably not as close as they appear. NYK, Clevland, Wolves, Pacers, etc…

My 2024-2025 Predictions:

  • Celtics: No surprises here. They check 2 of the 3 boxes and are one of the best-assembled rosters in years.
  • Nuggets: They still have arguably the best player in the league. If you have “that” guy and even just a competent roster, you can win a title.
  • Mavericks: Luka is now entering his prime. He and Kyrie figured out how to play together. They showed they can play defense in the playoffs. Also, Luka is in his prime. Prime Luka. 
  • 76ers: Emotionally, I’m not buying it because of health issues, but they check a lot of boxes. If somehow they find themselves healthy in the playoffs, this team poses significant issues for defenses.
  • OKC: My not-so-dark horse pick. They fall outside of the average age, but their shooting was insanely good last year and they upgraded the roster without losing much.
