Who is the best Defenders in each position? (Guard/Wing/Bigs)

We often discussed about Offensive players. But what about defensive players. So let’s go position by position.

Since Position is too fluid now. Let’s use a more advanced position label in Guard/Wing/Big

Who will you trust the most in guarding the best Guards in the league?

My answer will be Jrue Holiday. I think he is the best in containing flashy and shifty guards.

Who will you trust the most in guarding the best Wings in the league?

My answer will be Herb Jones. The only Non Big Man Who is included in All Nba First Defensive Team.

Who will you trust the most in guarding the best Bigs in the league?

This is tough. With all due respect to Gobert, Bam, and Wemby, AD.

I think Embiid is the best in defending Bigs inside the Block.

What do you think? How about yours?
