My First NBA Game

My first nba game was in the 2005 season, in new orleans. This was pre katrina, and we drove from baton to the game. The hornets were facing off against Lebron and the cavaliers. I was just seven, but Lebron’s 44 point bomb amazed me. I was only seven and drew gooden made the game tying layup, but it was an and1 so he got the free throw for the win.
