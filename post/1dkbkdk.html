[Murray] “There was a lot of mercenary feelings…the trade deadline is Thursday after they get home from [the Grammy trip]. No one got traded, but there were guys who were asking to get traded from a team that came home from the trip with the best record in the Western Conference & that felt weird.”

Quote from Law Murray, beat reporter for the Clippers

I don’t think there is a team in the league with as much talent and as much accomplishment that it just felt like they weren’t going to get too excited or too upset over the downs of a season, but there was a lot of ‘mercenary feelings’.

I was with the team on the Grammy trip, and they were outstanding. They emptied the tank on that trip: they won 6 out of 7 on an incredibly difficult trip as far as logistics are concerned. And the trade deadline is Thursday after they get home from that. No one got traded, but there were guys who were asking to get traded from a team that came home from the trip with the best record in the Western Conference, and that felt weird.

It felt very strange to me that you got this team that could do something very special, has a bunch of guys who have not accomplished what Kawhi has as far as being a champion, and then you got third stringers that were like “Yeah, we don’t want to be here,” and were making it so public that the game before the All-Star break they had to send guys home to get their minds right.
