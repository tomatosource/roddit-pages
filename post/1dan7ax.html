Kristaps Porzingis seems like the perfect 3rd option

It’s a head scratcher why Porzingis wasn’t more coveted around the league. I understand the injury issues, but if you can manage his minutes, he is a pivotal piece for any team.

He’s 7’2 with a 7’6 wingspan and can pull up from Steph Curry range. He can shoot the midrange as well, back his opponent down, and use his size effectively inside. On the defensive end, he’s extremely mobile for his height and a very good rim protector without picking up too many fouls.

Porzingis is like Kevin Love if he was 5 inches taller. He also has games like yesterday when he looks like a fusion of Karl-Anthony Towns and Rudy Gobert. He’s such a versatile player.
