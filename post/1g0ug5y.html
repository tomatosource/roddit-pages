Have any basketball lovers watched “The First Slam Dunk” on Netflix?

Just wanted to raise visibility on an incredible animated basketball movie called “The First Slam Dunk” that’s on Netflix. Watched it last night and was blown away. If you love basketball, definitely go check it out. Touches on a lot of nostalgic elements of endlessly practicing basketball as a kid, playing on a team and the bonds you make with your teammates, and having the resilience to win. Still thinking about it and wanted to share with other basketball fans, since the movie doesn’t seem that popular at all.
