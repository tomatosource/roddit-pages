Chris Paul deserves more credit for being an offensive engine that is also a positive defender

Lebron is often praised for being the only guy like this and he is definitely the better player and defender relative to CP3.

But Chris Paul deserves more love for being one of the rare guys in league history that can be a really effective high load offensive player while also having a decently strong positive impact defensively. Event though there are guys that are capable of both, often times when there is a massive burden offensively the defense falls apart. Or if they are really great defenders(usually bigs) they don’t have the playmaking or efficiency to consistently lead great offenses.

Over the course of his career all kinds of defensive metrics have loved him, even though he is a small guard.
