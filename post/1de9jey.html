Michael Jordan, LeBron James release statements honoring Jerry West

LeBron James tweeted:

Will truly miss our convos my dear friend! My thoughts and prayers goes out to your wonderful family! Forever love Jerry! Rest in Paradise my guy!

and

My mentor, My friend! Hopefully I continue to make you proud! 🥲. You’re already missed!

Tweet one, Tweet two

Michael Jordan texted Stephen A. Smith a statement which he read on First Take:

I am so deeply saddened at the news of Jerry’s passing. He was truly a friend and a mentor, like an older brother to me. I valued his friendship and knowledge. I always wished I could’ve played against him as a competitor, but the more I came to know him, I wish I had been his teammate. I admired his basketball insights, and he and I shared many similarities in how we approached the game. He will be forever missed. My condolences to his wife Karen and his sons. RIP Logo.

Source

Side note, people often scoff at Stephen A. Smith’s claims of having notable athletes text him and what not, but there’s no way he just made this up. It’s interesting that he messaged SAS directly and had him deliver the statement.
