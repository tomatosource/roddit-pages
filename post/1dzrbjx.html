Ish Wainright signs with Hapoel Tel-Aviv after few seasons in the NBA.

Source

He signed for two seasons.

According to the tweet, he also said (after signing): “I’m happy to be a part in this organization. I cannot wait to get on the court, start to work with my other teammates, and to bring wins for this club and the amazing fans of Hapoel Tel-Aviv”

Ish played mostly for the Suns and 7 games for the Trail Blazers.

Wainright was born in the USA, but represents Uganda.

Hapoel Tel-Aviv play in the EuroCup (a tier below the EuroLeague)
