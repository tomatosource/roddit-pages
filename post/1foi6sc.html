This sub is a Streamable graveyard now and it’s a real bummer

Sometimes when I can’t sleep I like to search random player names in this sub and watch highlights/read highlight threads from previous seasons.

Nearly every single Streamable highlight ever posted in this sub has been deleted, making it basically impossible to rewatch any specific highly upvoted moments from the past.

Seriously, go do an experiment: look up your favorite player or team from the last few years and try to find a single highlight post where the video still works. 95% of them are gone, essentially erasing the history of those hype plays and making the threads difficult to follow because the context has been deleted.

I realize there’s a “be the change” dynamic here but I’d like to encourage the amazing highlight posters that make this sub so fun to use a different video hosting service. This is also probably an NBA copyright strike issue, so I don’t know if there’s actually a solution but it’s such a bummer that so many incredible moments are basically impossible to watch again without rewatching entire games.
