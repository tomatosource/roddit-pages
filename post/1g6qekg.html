“There are two new names to add to a short list of candidates to become the next owner of the Celtics.

Mark Bezos, founding partner of HighPost Capital private equity group and half-brother of Amazon founder Jeff Bezos, has expressed interest as well, an industry source told the Globe.

The formal sale process only got started at the beginning of this month, with banking companies JPMorgan Chase and BDT & MSD facilitating the bidding on the Celtics’ behalf.”
