Do you think any records will be broken in this year’s Finals?

These are some notable (and not so notable lol) all-time records

Most in a finals game:

  • Points: 61 pts (Elgin Baylor, 1962 Finals)
  • Rebounds: 40 reb (Bill Russell, 1960 and 1962 Finals)
  • Assists: 21 ast (Magic Johnson, 1984 Finals)
  • Blocks: 9 blk (Dwight Howard, 2009 Finals)
  • Steals: 7 stl (Robert Horry, 1995 Finals)
  • Turnovers: 10 tov (Magic Johnson, 1980 Finals)
  • Minutes played: 62 min (Kevin Johnson, 1993 Finals)
  • 3-pointers made: 9 3pm (Stephen Curry, 2018 Finals)
  • Field goals made: 22 fgm (Rick Barry, 1967 Finals and Elgin Baylor, 1962 Finals)
  • Free throws made: 21 ftm (Dwyane Wade, 2006 Finals)
  • 3-pointers attempted: 17 3pa (Stephen Curry, 2018 Finals)
  • Field goals attempted: 48 fga (Rick Barry, 1967 Finals)
  • Free throws attempted: 39 fta (Shaquille O’Neal, 2000 Finals)
  • Field goals attempted without a single make: 14 fga (Dennis Johnson, 1978 Finals and Chick Reiser, 1948 Finals)
  • Field goals attempted without a single miss: 11 fga (Scott Wedman, 1985 Finals)
  • 3-pointers attempted without a single make: 11 3pa (John Starks, 1994 Finals)
  • 3-pointers attempted without a single miss: 5 3pa (Mike Miller and Danny Green, 2013 Finals)
  • Free throws attempted without a single make: 5 fta (Tristan Thompson, 2016 Finals)
  • Free throws attempted without a single miss: 8 fta (AC Green, 1988 Finals)

Most in a finals debut (that I could find):

  • Points: 48 pts (Allen Iverson, 2001 Finals)
  • Rebounds: 25 reb (Wilt Chamberlain, 1964 Finals)
  • Assists: 14 ast (Nikola Jokic, 2023 Finals)
  • Blocks: 7 blk (Patrick Ewing, 1994 Finals)
  • Steals: 6 stl (Steve Mix, 1977 Finals)
  • Turnovers: 6 tov (LeBron James, 2007 Finals and Gus Williams, 1978 Finals)

Team records:

  • Most points in a game: 148 pts (Boston Celtics, 1985 Finals)
  • Most assists in a game: 44 ast (Los Angeles Lakers, 1970 Finals and 1987 Finals)
  • Most rebounds in a game: 93 reb (Philadelphia 76ers, 1967 Finals)
  • Least points in a game: 54 pts (Utah Jazz, 1998 Finals)
  • Largest margin of victory in a game: 96-54 (Chicago Bulls, 1998 Finals)
  • Most overtime periods in a game: 3 OT (Boston Celtics vs Phoenix Suns, 1976 Finals and Chicago Bulls vs Phoenix Suns, 1993 Finals)
  • Most steals in a game: 18 stl (Boston Celtics, 2008 Finals)
  • Most turnovers in a game: 36 tov (Golden State Warriors, 1975 Finals)
  • Most blocks in a game: 14 blk (Los Angeles Lakers, 2010 Finals)
  • Most fouls in a game: 42 pf (Minneapolis Lakers, 1950 Finals)
  • Most 3-pointers made in a game: 24 (Cleveland Cavaliers, 2017 Finals)
  • Most 3-pointers attempted in a game: 47 (Los Angeles Lakers, 2020 Finals)

I think the following records could be broken:

Team 3PA (Celtics - likely. We attempted 47 or more threes 26 times this season as well as in game 1 of the first round.) points in a finals debut (Luka - possible. We’re probably gonna guard him 1 on 1 and try to limit the other guys, so look for him to have a huge scoring night in his first finals game.), FGA without a miss (Gafford or Lively - possible. I think one of them is very likely to at least come close to the record.), FTA without a miss (Multiple - possible. I think there are a number of guys on either team that are very capable of going 9⁄9 or better.).

What do you guys think?
