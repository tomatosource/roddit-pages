He fully intended to retire a Clipper, but the Sixers complicated matters when they insisted on trading PJ Tucker in any Harden deal. LA had no interest in Tucker, so they spent a while looking for a third team to take on Tucker in the trade that would also allow them to keep Batum, but were unable to find one.
