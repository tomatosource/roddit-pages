LeBron’s Cashier’s Check He Might Want To Claim

Searching through the LifeProTips subreddit I came across the ability to search for unclaimed money. So naturally knowing I had none (actually did have $30 from more than a decade ago) I thought “Surely some famous person has a ton of unclaimed money that they don’t even know about.”

LeBron James was my first search, and I’ll be darned, he has a $87,200 cashier’s check unclaimed. Imagine needing or getting that check and not vehemently searching for it to appear. I can’t be 100% sure that it’s his, but I don’t know a lot of other LeBron James’ rolling around California that might be missing an 87k check and not knowing it.

I thought it might be necessary to state that it would constitute fraud to try and do so FOR LeBron for those idiots out there thinking “How hard could it be?”.

It would definitely be worth looking into for a check that size. If any of you lurkers out there could just DM your homie LeBron I’m sure he’d be pumped to hear from you.

For all my  nephews out there, just give your name a search and see what pops up, you never know. In the original post it said roughly 1⁄7 have unclaimed money.
