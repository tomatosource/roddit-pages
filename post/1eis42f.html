You know what sports game would be perfect right now? A NBA JAM.

Lately, I’ve been replaying NBA JAM: On Fire Edition and every time I play the game, I think to myself… “why isn’t there a new one?”

EA still has the rights for it, but y’know they’re too lazy to actually try something. The world needs another over the top basketball game ( I have an opinion where I think we should leave NBA Street in the past, that’s a different story for another day. Also, NBA 2K Playgrounds 2 is dogshit. )

The NBA right now is packed with so many duos like LeBron & AD, Luka & Kyrie, Maxey & Embiid, Dame & Giannis, KD & Book, the list goes on. It’s legit a perfect time. Also, with the current state of the WNBA? It’ll be a perfect addition to the game. Add some wacky characters in the mix just like the other games.

The remake wouldn’t even need that much improvements. Same gameplay and features as NBA JAM: On Fire Edition. Bring back remix mode and boss battles from the 2010 NBA Jam. A much bigger roster, and the return of Tim Kitzrow. It’s a perfect arcade basketball experience.
