Revisiting 2006 Playoffs: The Unique Season in 2000s

Why Unique?

In 2000s. West Champions are always either Lakers or Spurs. For east, it is more diverse with Pacers, Nets, Heat, Celtics having a taste. But usually it was Pistons who came on top.

But in 2006, it is the only year in the decade where Neither Lakers nor Spurs made the finals. Interestingly, Pistons didn’t make it in the east too.

2006,

Kobe’s Lakers was stunned by Nash’s Suns in the first round of WCF. So everybody was expecting spurs to represent west. But Dirk’s Mavs had other plans. Beating Spurs in a very tough 4-3 7 game series in WCF.

At the east side, Pistons was coming from a back to back finals appearance in 04 and 05. (Winning it all in 04). Just when people thought, they will make the finals. A rising star in Dwyane Wade lead the heat to stunning Pistons. (Btw, Old Shaq joined Heat as we all know)

And there we go, we had an unexpected

Mavs VS Heat in the Finals at 06.No one expected that before the 06 season started.
