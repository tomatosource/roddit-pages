There are only two number 6’s left in the entire NBA

The number 6 was retired league-wide a couple years ago to honor Bill Russell which obviously means there can be no new #6’s. The players that had the number before its retirement were able to keep it, but this offseason we’ve already lost a couple with Alex Caruso and most recently Jordan McLaughlin going to new teams.

By my count, DeAndre Jordan and Jaylin Williams are the last remaining #6’s in the NBA; the last two players who will EVER wear the number 6 in this league. I don’t know which of them will be the last 6 standing, but my money’s on Jwill.
