Vince Carter becomes the 16th player to have his jersey retired by multiple teams.

Prior to the Raptors announcement, there were 15 players to have their jersey number retired by multiple teams (not including Bill Russell who had his number retired league-wide). Now Carter will have his jersey retired by both the Nets and the Raptors. Here’s the list of the other players to have their jersey retired by multiple teams.
