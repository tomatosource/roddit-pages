The 2024 men’s Olympic basketball team has faced 22 rotational NBA players in just five exhibition games. The 1992 Dream Team only faced 21 fringe NBA players during their entire tenure.

Throughout the 1992 Dream Team’s entire tenure (the Tournament of the Americas and the Olympics), they only faced the following 21 players, who were all at the very least fringe NBA players: Lazaro Borrell, Andrés Guibert, Leo Rautins, Bill Wennington, Mike Smrek, Stuart Gray, Mario Butler, Jose Ortiz, Ramon Rivas, Carl Herrera, Dražen Petrović, Toni Kukoc, Zan Tabak, Stojko Vranković, Dino Rada, Uwe Blab, Detlef Schrempf, João Vianna, Rolando Ferreira, Arvydas Sabonis, Sarunas Marčiulionis.

“Fringe” NBA player is defined as having played at least one game in the NBA.

In the last five exhibition games, the 2024 team has faced the following rotational players: RJ Barrett, Dillon Brooks, Dwight Powell, Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, Jamal Murray, Kelly Olynyk, Lu Dort, Trey Lyles, Andrew Nembhard, Nickeil Alexander-Walker, Dyson Daniels, Jock Landale, Josh Giddey, Josh Green, Duop Reath, Joe Ingles, Nikola Jokic, Franz Wagner, Mo Wagner, Daniel Theis, Dennis Schroder, Wenyen Gabriel.

“Rotational” NBA player is defined as having played at least 58 games (the league minimum for statistical awards) in the last season or the previous one.
