Spain announces final 12-man roster for the Paris Olympics, Alex Abrines replaces Juan Nunez

Source

Spain has announced their final 12-man roster for the Paris Olympics. The roster is the same as the one who played at the Olympic Qualifying Tournament in Valencia, with the only exception being that former Thunder wing Alex Abrines replaces this year’s Spurs draft pick Juan Nunez.

Alex Abrines missed the OQT due to an injury.

The roster is spearheaded by Grizzlies’ forward Santi Aldama, this year’s All-Euroleague selection Lorenzo Brown, the reigning EuroBasker MVP Willy Hernangomez, this year’s Euroleague champion Juancho Hernangomez and Warriors’ big man Usman Garuba.

It’s completed by two veterans and Spanish basketball legends Rudy Fernandez and Sergio Llull, last year’s All-Champions League First Team selection Dario Brizuela, this year’s FIBA Champions League champion Alberto Diaz, the Valencia duo of Jaime Pradilla and Xabier Lopez-Arostegui, and the before mentioned Alex Abrines.

Spain will play in the same group as Canada, Greece and Australia at the Olympics.
