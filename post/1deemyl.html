Jerry West’s (first) Hall of Fame induction was closer to the formation of the NBA than to the present day.

Jerry West was first initiated into the Basketball Hall of Fame in 1980 for his achievements as a player and again in 2010 as a member of the 1960 Summer Olympic team.

Given that his first induction was in 1980 (44 years ago), that means that it’s closer in time to the 1936 Olympics, WWII (1939), and the formation of the NBA (as the Basketball Association of America; 1946) than to the present day.

His lifelong achievements are legendary.
