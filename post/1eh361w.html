[OC]All 5 of the Boston Celtics’ starters descend from the 2013 Kevin Garnett + Paul Pierce trade with the Brooklyn Nets

On July 12, 2013, the Boston Celtics traded Kevin Garnett, Paul Pierce, Jason Terry, DJ White, a 2017 First Round Pick, and a 2017 Second Round Pick to the Brooklyn Nets in exchange for Keith Bogans, MarShon Brooks, Kris Humphries, Kris Joseph, Gerald Wallace, and first round draft picks in 2014, 2016, 2017, and 2018. All 5 of the Celtics current starters were traded to the Celtics for assets that were directly acquired from this trade.

Jaylen Brown

The 2016 1st Round Pick that became Jaylen Brown was acquired by the Celtics in the Garnett-Pierce trade.

Jayson Tatum

A 2017 1st Round Pick that eventually became Markelle Fultz was acquired in the Garnett-Pierce trade.

3 days before the 2017 draft, that pick was traded for the 2017 First that would eventually become Jayson Tatum and the 2019 First that became Romeo Langford

Derrick White

That same 2017 1st Round Pick that eventually became Markelle Fultz was acquired in the Garnett-Pierce trade.

As I stated before, the 2019 First that became Romeo Langford was acquired in exchange for the Markelle Fultz pick.

Romeo Langford was drafted by the Celtics and then traded a couple years later with Josh Richardson and a 2022 First for Derrick White

Kristaps Porzingis

The Boston Celtics acquired MarShon Brooks in the Garnett-Pierce trade

MarShon Brooks and Jordan Crawford were traded for Joel Anthony and three Second Rounders, one being a 2016 Second Rounder that became Deyonta Davis

Deyonta Davis was traded on draft night with Rade Zagorac to Memphis for the 2019 First that became Matisse Thybulle

In June 2019, Thybulle was traded on draft night to the Sixers for Carsen Edwards and Ty Jerome

Ty Jerome was flipped a couple weeks later with Aaron Baynes to Phoenix for the 2020 First that became Desmond Bane.

Desmond Bane was drafted by the Celtics and then traded with Enes Katner on draft night for a 2023 Second and a 2025 Second.

In February 2023, that 2023 Second was traded with Justin Jackson and a 2029 Second to OKC for Mike Muscala

In June 2023, Mike Muscala was traded with Danilo Gallinari, Julian Phillips, and Marcus Smart for Kristaps Porzingis, Marcus Sasser, and the 2024 first that became Bub Carrington.

Jrue Holiday

Follow the Kristaps Porzingis line and you see that the 2024 First that became Bub Carrington was acquired in the Mike Muscala deal.

In June 2023, that 2024 First was traded with Malcolm Brogdon, Robert Williams, and a 2029 First for Jrue Holiday.

~~~~~~~~

As you can see, all five of the Celtics starters were traded for in exchange for assets that were in some way acquired from the Kevin Garnett-Paul Pierce trade in 2013. The trade definitely has a case for being one of the best in history (or worst if you’re a Nets fan).

Note: Jordan Walsh is the only other current Celtic that also descends from this trade. That goes like this: Follow Porzingis line to Mike Muscala, then Marcus Sasser, to James Nnaji, to Colby Jones, to Jordan Walsh.
