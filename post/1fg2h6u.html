Does Embiid and the 76ers have the most pressure to perform in the playoffs?

The 76ers had an amazing offseason. They have had really good teams surrounding Embiid as well with the Butler era, Harden era, and now the PG era. Embiid has proven himself as a MVP contender for years and recently won a MVP in 2023. Yet they never made it past the 2nd round of the Eastern Conference Playoffs. Compared to his peers: Jokic, Giannis, Tatum, Luka; Embiid is the only one who hasn’t made it past the 2nd round and 1 out of 2 that hasn’t won a championship in a league full of parity.

But the 76ers are not bad at all. They have a legitimate and arguably one of best big 3 that can compete with any other contending team’s big 3.

Embiid/PG/Maxey

Tatum/Brown/Holiday

Giannis/Lillard/Middleton

Jokic/Murray/Gordon

Luka/Kyrie/Klay

SGA/Chet/Williams

Edwards/KAT/Gobert

Brunson/Randle/Bridges

On paper, the 76ers look good. But on paper, the Clippers in the PG/Kawhi was one of the best as well. They never achieved success. Are the 76ers doomed to repeat as the Clippers of the East? Failure to ever win a championship.
