[Thinking Basketball] 2024 NBA Finals Preview

Some nice snippets from today’s pod that I think provide some interesting base for discussion.

On their overall feelings towards the series, at 15:06:

I feel like Boston is a really tough matchup for Dallas. The style makes the fight, and so especially in the West, where we just saw some crazy different styles matching up […describing Dallas/OKC, Denver/MIN…] and then we get to the conference finals, and it’s a totally different match up for both of those teams than what we just saw, and how that influences our perceptions of our teams. My assumption is there is a little bit of anti Boston, there’s a bit of Luka-mania, because he’s such an exciting, dynamic player and there’s a little bit of recency bias”.

On Kristaps’ ability to cause problems for Dallas’s defense, at 33:57:

When Porzingis has that spacing or Al Horford, he’s asking Derrick Lively or Daniel Gafford, are you going to sit in the paint or are you going to let me shoot a 3? And if he doesn’t sit in the paint, then you potentially take away some of this insane shot blocking, defense, rim protection that we’ve seen from Dallas.

On the disparity of predictions ranging from a short Boston series win to short Dallas series win, at 45:46:

It’s very rare to go into an NBA finals series where at least from the data, both teams look very good. Yes, you can say that Boston looks better kind of across the board, but Dallas has played at a similar level and obviously just went through a harder Western Conference run. It’s very rare to get there and see, Boston in 5, Boston 5, Dallas 5, Dallas 4, usually you would expect some to be more like a 7 game series. But it seems polarizing in terms of how people are coming at this.

Thoughts?
