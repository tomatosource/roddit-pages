Who would you compare Rob Dillingham?

Standing at around 6 foot 3 Rob definitely has a lot of potential to be something big in the league, he’s decently athletic with an interesting game style which attracts a lot of attention especially to the youth. He will definitely show up in years to come if given the right opportunity. And as previously mentioned his way of playing the game attracts youth players making him extremely marketable now and in the future. He has great ball handling as expected for a 6”3 guard, his playmaking is definitely already in the works, he has a great touch, his midrange shot paired with his isolation scoring is elite, and most importantly he is confident in his game as he doesn’t shy away from making mistakes which is important for rookie. Though his lack of efficient shot selection and his consistency in the defensive end with his thin frame will definitely be a component he’ll need to work on this year. But then again what do I know? I’m not in the league, I simply observe what I see as a basketball fan and then I try to analyse it the way I see it. But obviously him being a rookie I am not expecting too much of him this year especially being in a team like Minnesota as they have the likes of AE, Kat, Naz Reid, Jayden Mcdaniels Alexander Walker, etc. BUT with that being said watching his highlights and full games from NCAA, OTE, and recently NBA Summer league I can as of now say his game reminds me of a Bones Hyland (LAC).
