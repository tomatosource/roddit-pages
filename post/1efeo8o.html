Blazers beat reporter says the Blazers goal is to trade one of Anfernee Simons or Jerami Grant before training camp.

Link to clip: https://x.com/sactown1140/status/1818055243160326323?s=46

I think this is an interesting piece of news that if true, confirms the Blazers will be making a major trade his offseason. This will help the Blazers completely commit to a rebuild and tank for Cooper Flagg or other prospects in a perceived great draft class.

The Lakers have been heavily linked to trading for Jerami Grant recently as many Lakers reporters have confirmed the Lakers maintain interest in Grant. Zach Lowe’s comments that they should start printing Jerami Grant Lakers jerseys triggered a significant amount of the most recent speculation.

As a Lakers fan, I would be very happy to trade for him if the Blazers ease off their two first round picks stance. Unfortunately, I don’t think they will because that is what they want and they have leverage in these negotiations. They can wait until the deadline, next offseason or the one after if necessary to trade him and try get good value.

Simons would be a good player for a contending team as well. If his value is decent, I wouldn’t mind trading for him as well.

There has been much more noise about Grant getting traded than Simons lately, so I think Grant is the more likely player to be traded by the Blazers this offseason.
