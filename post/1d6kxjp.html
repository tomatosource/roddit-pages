Horford Rest Factor

As much as KP coming back excites me, Horford entering these finals having played so few minutes is almost as big of a deal in my opinion.

Horford entering 2022 finals - 624 playoff minutes

Horford 2023 playoffs - 617 playoff minutes

Horford 2024 playoffs - 425 playoff minutes

And the CF is the most grueling series as the games are all every other day, whereas the finals mostly have two days off in between each game.

Those nearly 200 fewer minutes played than through 2022 CF are huge.

Only 3 days off between end of Heat and beginning of Warriors series in 2022 vs 9 days off between series this year. That’s massive.

And Celtics rested Al 4 of the last 5 games of the regular season. Which we didn’t do in 2022. Al played 5 of the last 6 games that season.
