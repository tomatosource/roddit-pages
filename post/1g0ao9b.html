The Memphis Grizzlies and Atlanta Hawks are sleeper teams to keep an eye on for Bruce Brown, Sports Illustrated has learned.

Worth noting that this insider from Sports Illustrated had previously reported interest from the New York Knicks and the Dallas Mavericks, two teams which are known to have been interested in the NBA champion around the time the Toronto Raptors acquired him as part of the Pascal Siakam trade so there is some credibility to both the Hawks and Grizzlies being interested. They also reported on the Lakers recent interest in Knicks center Mitchell Robinson, amongst other rumors regarding the ongoing Mitchell Robinson trade saga™.

The Hawks almost certainly would view a Bruce Brown trade as dumping De’Andre Hunter’s contract (Clint Capela is expiring and the Raptors already have Jakob Poeltl) though what one would view as fair compensation for one of the more quietly bad contracts in the league is not clear.

For the Grizzlies, Brandon Clarke would be required in a Bruce Brown trade just for salary matching purposes.
