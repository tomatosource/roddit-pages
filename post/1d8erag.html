Hoop Dreams (best film of 1994)

Since we are all waiting for the finals to begin, I thought I’d share one of the best basketball films ever made, Hoop Dreams.

It makes me think, all those hours spent in the gym, until closing time, running suicides, practicing boring drills, playing against older players, getting my ass kicked, dreaming of the NBA, not to make it.

But looking back how much I enjoyed the process.

Really puts it into perspective how good these NBA and Euroleague players are.

Every gym across America, backwaters USA or Europe , just to make it to the top league.

Sometimes, no matter how much work you put in, you just won’t make it.

But you can enjoy it after.
