The rookies are being underrated in this draft

Ryan Dunn on the Suns, Reed Sheppard on the Rockets, Stephon Castle on the Spurs, Dillingham on the Wolves, Risacher on the Hawks, Edey on the Grizzlies, etc. I’m looking at these rookies and how theyre so far playing and if most of their play styles translate to the regular season, these rookies could be small yet important pieces that have the ability to change the entire identity of a team for the better. Whether if they’re a starter, or coming off the bench and giving that team more depth.

I feel like this rookie class is about to shock some people by how much they could potentially affect a team. Are there any rookies that will look like full-blown stars this season? Probably not. (And I know it’s preseason), but we have rookies playing great defense and putting up 15-20 points a night on good efficiency right now against real NBA players. Of course, we have to wait for the regular season to start to see if it all translates, but this has been a super promising rookie class so far. I don’t remember so many rookies last year giving these kinds of performances in the preseason every other night. It was mostly just 2 or 3 rookies last year that looked super promising at this time during the preseason.
