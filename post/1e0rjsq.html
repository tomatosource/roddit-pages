The French national team will retire Tony Parker’s number 9 jersey at tomorrow’s preparation game against Serbia in Lyon

Source

Tony Parker has played 181 games for France (11th all-time) and scored 2741 points (4th all-time).

During the youth ranks he led France to the U18 EuroBasket gold in 2000, winning the tournament MVP in the process.

He has played in eight EuroBaskets for the senior national team, winning the gold in 2013, in which he also was the tournament’s MVP and top scorer, the silver in 2011 (top scorer) and the bronze twice, in 2005 and 2015. He broke the record for most total points scored at the EuroBaskets, surpassing the legendary Nikos Galis in 2015, before his record was also broken later on by Pau Gasol.

He also played for France at the 2012 and 2016 Olympics.

This will be the first time that the French national team retire anyone’s jersey and the second time a team retire Parker’s jersey, as the Spurs already did so in 2019.
