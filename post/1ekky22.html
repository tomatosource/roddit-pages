Who are your franchise’s GOAT, BOAT, and Captain?

Greatest of All-Time, as in the player who is the most memorable and had the most impact on basketball wearing your team’s jersey, the Best of All-Time, as in the best player period when wearing your team’s jersey, and Captain as in who you’d pick to set the culture and lead the team into battle without having to be the best or greatest.

For the Nets…

GOAT: Julius Erving

BOAT: Kevin Durant (although based on what Durant says I reserve the right to replace him with Dr. J here at any time)

CAPTAIN: Jason Kidd

I think this is a pretty interesting question for certain fanbases and can divide up how we rank players. Like for example, for the NBA as a whole, you might say GOAT: Jordan, BOAT: Lebron, CAPTAIN: Russell. Maybe.
