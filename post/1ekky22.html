Who are your franchise’s GOAT, BOAT, and Captain?

Greatest of All-Time, as in the player who is the most memorable and had the most impact on basketball wearing your team’s jersey, the Best of All-Time, as in the best player period when wearing your team’s jersey (Nets can’t claim Kevin Garnett, Raptors can’t claim Hakeem etc), and Captain as in who you’d pick to set the culture and lead the team into battle without having to be the best or greatest.

BOAT: Kevin Durant (although based on what Durant says I reserve the right to replace him with Dr. J here at any time)
