Wemby is the only player qualified to the quarter finals to lead his team in points, assist and rebounds. (Jokic is very close but Bogdanovic has 0.3ppg more than him).

Team France stats leader :

  • Points : 1) Wemby (17pg), 2) Batum (10.7)
  • Assists : 1) Wemby (3pg), 2) Albicy (2.7),
  • Rebounds : 1) Wemby (10.7pg), 2) Gobert (6.3)

Wemby is second in turnover (2.7pg) behind Gobert (3pg).

Wemby has also the most minutes (30.2mpg) behind Batum (31.7) and above Gobert (21.2).
