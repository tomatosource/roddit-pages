Victor Wembanyama is leading Team France in points, assists, rebounds, steals and 3pts made in the Olympics (and other interesting stats)

(This is not in response to the Mo Wagner post btw, I was planning to make this post before checking the sub)

Wemby is the only player qualified to the quarter finals to lead his team in points, assist and rebounds. (Jokic is very close but Bogdanovic has 0.3ppg more than him).

Team France stats leader :

  • Points : 1) Wemby (17pg), 2) Batum (10.7)
  • Assists : 1) Wemby (3pg), 2) Albicy (2.7),
  • Rebounds : 1) Wemby (10.7pg), 2) Gobert (6.3)

Wemby is second in turnover (2.7pg) behind Gobert (3pg).

Wemby has also the most minutes (30.2mpg) behind Batum (31.7) and above Gobert (21.2).

Victor is shooting at 43.8% from the 3pts line (7-16 cumulated). He made one more 3ts than Batum (6-17). This is very small sample size for shooting, so I took a look at the exhibition games from this summer. From 5 games, he shot 13-32 (40.6%) from the 3pts line. In total, Wemby is shooting at 41.6% this summer at 6 attempts per game. During his rookie season, he was shooting from 3 at 32.5% from 5.5 attempts per game.
