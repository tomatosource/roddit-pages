What’s the best hypothetical starting 5 that could have ACTUALLY existed?

But obviously this could have never existed in real life - even if LeBron was in the NBA at 16, Jordan just wasn’t good enough to be in an all-time team in his Wizards days. Curry/Shaq is another popular duo that wouldn’t work as their primes never intersected.

So what’s the best hypothetical of starting 5s that ACTUALLY could have existed, with all their peaks/primes intersecting?
