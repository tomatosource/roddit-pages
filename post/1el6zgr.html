1v1 Basketball as an Olympic Sport Would be Crazy

1v1 is such a large part of basketball culture. Making it an Olympic sport only makes sense. If you can have 10 different events for swimming and track, it only makes sense 1v1 gets added to basketball.

Separate into a few height/position separated divisions, and have one open division. It would get hell of a lot of views. And most importantly, put no restrictions on NBA athletes competing!

This would change basketball culture forever and an Olympic gold as a 1v1 would be highly sought out from NBA players as it’s a prestigious achievement. Unlike the idea for All star game 1v1, where the reward is zero but the risk is high.
