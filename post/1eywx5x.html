If Jrue Holiday retired today, would he have had a better career than Chauncey Billups?

Both are NBA Champions. Both are leaders and class teammates. Both are ceiling raisers when added to already good teams (Billups in Detroit/Denver and Holiday in Milwaukee/Boston). Both have played well into their 30s and provide elite defence and leadership even when unable to score as they aged.

Is it crazy to say that Jrue Holiday could retire having a better career than Chauncey Billups did, and was even better than he was in his prime?

Chauncey Billups (1996-2012)

  • 1x NBA champion (2004)

  • 1x NBA Finals MVP (2004)

  • 5× NBA All-Star (2006–2010)

  • 1x All-NBA Second Team (2006)

  • 2× All-NBA Third Team (2007, 2009)

  • 2× NBA All-Defensive Second Team (2005, 2006)

  • 1x NBA Sportsmanship Award (2009)
