1. LeBron (40,490) and Bronny James (0) with 40,490 points

3. Dell (12,670) and Stephen Curry (23,668) with 36,338 points

4. Mychal (12,810) and Klay Thompson (15,531) with 28,341 points

5. Dolph (18,438) and Danny Schayes (8,780) with 27,218 points
