There are only 20 NBA players left who played in the league before 2010

With the season starting in two days, I was curious (i.e. bored) and did some BBRef research. Out of the ~400 players currently signed to a team, only 20 players played at least one NBA game before 01/01/10. The oldest is (of course) LeBron, at 39. The youngest is Jrue Holiday, at 34.

If you’re counting those on a team before 01/01/10, the number goes up to 21 - Patty Mills, currently on the Jazz, was drafted by Portland in 2009 but only played his first game on 01/04/10.

Also of note are Garrett Temple and Joe Ingles, who both went undrafted in 2009 but played their first NBA games after 2010 (02/09/10 and 10/29/14 respectively). And of course there are a couple free agents like Robin Lopez who could be signed this season that would up the number.

(FWIW: There are a couple Euros who played overseas before 2010 as well, such as Jonas Valančiūnas. Mentioning these so pedants don’t act like I forgot them)
