[OC] If the NBA awarded Gold, Silver and Bronze in the playoffs

Inspired by the recently concluded Olympics, I went back to the 1984 NBA Playoffs – where the league inaugurated the 16-team playoff format – and faux retroactively awarded Silver and Bronze Larry O’Brien trophies for the Finals runners-up and the CF runners-up, respectively.

Since there is no bronze medal series in the association, I just gave both the ECF and WCF finalists the bronze, just like how they do in Olympic boxing, where there is a field of roughly 20-30 competitors per weight class, just like how the league was back in ‘84 up to the present.

In the earlier versions of the NBA, back in the ‘60s and before that, the championship team only needed to win 2 series to be crowned the champions. In this current 16-team playoff format, the gold medal winner will have to win 4, the silver medalists need 3, and the bronze medalists with 2 series wins.
