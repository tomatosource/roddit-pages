Serbian national team head coach Svetislav Pesic on the US prior to the Olympics: “This (US) team is better than the (1992) Dream Team in Barcelona”

Source

“This team is stronger than the Dream Team in Barcelona.”

“I was there with the German team and then we thought there were no better players than these,” Pesic said. “This team that will appear in Paris will be stronger than the Dream Team, with the fact that not everyone will prepare just for them like not everyone was prepared just for the Dream Team in 1992. Although everyone wanted to take a picture with them. We don’t want that now.”

“It will be interesting to see how far non-NBA basketball has evolved compared to the NBA after all these years. That’s a big challenge for us, but our concetration remains on France, Canada, we don’t know if Italy, Puerto Rico or Lithuania will be playing in the same group as us, we need to win two games in Paris, that’s our focus. We’re not thinking about the matchup against the US.

Pesic has coached the German national team at the 1992 Olympics in Barcelona, where they lost to the US 111-68.

He also led Yugoslavia to the World Cup title in 2002 in Indianapolis, after beating the US team, led by Paul Pierce, Jermaine O’Neal and Ben Wallace, in the quarter-finals 81-78.

Serbia will be playing in the same group as the US at the Paris Olympics, as well as face off in a friendly in Abu Dhabi a few days prior.
