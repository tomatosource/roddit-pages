Would a bogdan/hunter for porter jr/nnaji swap make sense for both teams?

For Atlanta; they have a bit of a logjam of guys that are borderline starter level but not really going to put them in a direction other than average, they swap two starter level guys for a guy in MPJ who could potentially be a 20+ ppg scorer in a different scenario. By dropping two starter level guys they open up the roster to start Trae-Dyson-mpj-jalen-capela, which I think is actually a pretty fun roster which could rise, also opens up a lot of their bench minutes for the young guys, bufkin-rissacher-okongwu for example should all find minutes now

For Denver; they’re falling into the trap modern contenders can fall into where they pay all their proven starters big money and have little money left to bolster their roster. Alongside this issue, their front office appears unwilling to pay starters they see as expendable. I think this trade solves a lot of issues for them, porter is easily the most expendable of the Murray-Gordon-porter-jokic core, and in exchange for him they’re able to add two starter level guys to a thinned out roster. Hunter I believe would start, I think he couple be revamped on a jokic led roster and really boost his offensive game, will never be quite the shooter porter was but should be a defensive upgrade at least. And then bogdan they could either start too, or start Braun and bring bogdan off the bench which would do wonders for their bench adding a 6MOTY candidate.

I see this as a win win, what do y’all think
