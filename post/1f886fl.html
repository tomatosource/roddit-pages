Improving the award for Most Improved Player: Suggestions

#Background

Looking forward to the 2024-25 season, betting odds have opened up for various awards. One of the odds sticking out is the betting for Most Improved Player due to the inclusion of players such as Victor Wembanyama, Evan Mobley, and Jalen Williams. For those of you who cannot look at the article and see the players listed and there betting odds, here are the players listed below

  • Evan Mobley: +1200
  • Jonathan Kuminga: +1400

  • Scottie Barnes: +1600
  • Jalen Williams: +2000

  • Josh Giddey: +2500
  • Immanuel Quickley: +2500

This has lead to a lot of discussion regarding who should be in consideration of winning the award since there is a belief that Wemby should not be in consideration. Its hard to say that he should not be, though, because there isn’t an explicit criteria for who should qualify for the award. The best thing I could find was this definition from Wikipedia:

“The NBA’s Most Improved Player Award (MIP) is an annual National Basketball Association (NBA) award given to the player who has shown the most progress during the regular season compared to previous seasons”

In light of this controversy, below what I believe should be the criteria, so that this debate can finally be over.

#My Proposal

A player should not be considered for MIP if any of the criteria are met:

  • Player has won MVP in any of the previous three seasons or finished in the Top 5 of MVP voting
  • Player has won DPOY in any of the previous three seasons or finished in the Top 5 of DPOY voting
  • Player has won ROY in any of the previous three seasons or finished in the Top 2 of ROY voting
  • Player has achieved All-NBA (first, second, or third team) in any of the previous three seasons
  • Player has achieved All-Defense (first, second, or third team) in any of the previous three seasons
  • Player has achieved All-Rookie First Team the previous season
  • Player has finished in the Top 15 of what I call “sectional All-Star voting” ** By “sectional All-Star voting” I mean Top 15 All-Star fan voting for any of the “sections”. The four sections are Frontcourt Eastern Conference, Backcourt Eastern Conference, Frontcourt Western Conference, and Backcourt Western Conference

##Justification

The justification is that spirit of the award is

  • player you wrote off (Coby White, Lauri Markkanen, Jordan Poole)
  • player that genuinely surprised you because they were not on your radar as potential Top 50 player and turned into one (Desmond Bane, Victor Oladipo)

#Re-evaluating the Players From Above

With this in mind, lets see who would be eliminated from consideration based on the criteria we’ve laid out

  • Victor Wembanyama (because he was All-Defense in 2024)
  • Evan Mobley (because he was All-Defense in 2023)
  • Scottie Barnes (because he was Top 15 in voting for Frontcourt Eastern Conference in 2024)
  • Jalen Williams (because he was Top 15 in voting for Frontcourt Western Conference in 2024)
