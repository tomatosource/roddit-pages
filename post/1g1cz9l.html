What is the most historically fascinating/interesting team in NBA history in your opinion?

It could be bad, mediocre or great team.

My pick is the 1994 Denver Nuggets, 8th seed sub .500 record. Went down 0-2 against the Supersonics. Came back and won 3 straight games. Went down 0-3 against Jazz, won 3 straight games forced game 7. Unfortunately fell short in game 7. The first 8th seed to upset a first seed and almost clinching a conference finals berth. They had pre-DPOY winner Dikembe Mutombo and Mahmoud Abdul-Rauf who was 2 decades ahead of its time.

I don’t know much about the NBA, please educate me with more interesting teams!
