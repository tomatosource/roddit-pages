Stanley cup finals isn’t following the same script as the NBA Finals anymore as the Edmonton Oilers force a game 7 after being down 3-0 in the series to the Florida Panthers

The first 4 games followed the same path as the NBA finals where after being 3-0 down, both the Oilers and the Mavs blew the ever living shit out of their opponent to win game 4. Dallas ended up losing game 5 via blowout however the Edmonton Oilers won game 5 in Florida 5-3 and just won game 6 back in Edmonton blowing out the Panthers 5-1.

Should be a pretty historic game 7 on Monday. Only once has a 3-0 lead has been overcome in the Stanley cup finals which happened in 1942 where Toronto rallied back against Detroit to win it.

Also another random fact: Tatum and Tkachuk (Florida panthers) went to school together.
