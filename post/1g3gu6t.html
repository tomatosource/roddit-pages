The KAT / Julius Randle trade will go down as a rare win-win

Initially I thought this was a bizarre trade and not a good one for the Timberwolves. But there were 2 things I failed to realize:

  1. Divincenzo is really good. Maybe the best 3&D in the league considering only Curry and Doncic made more 3s last season (Doncic only made 1 more three than Divincenzo while Divincenzo shot 40% and Doncic 38%).

  2. Naz Reid is also really good and somewhat under the radar because I forgot about him when I first heard about this trade. Last season he took a big step upward and shot 41% from three on 5 attempts per game, adding pseudo-elite three point shooting to a good, well-rounded game he already had.

So if the wolves move Randle for a draft pick, or if they figure out an effective way to integrate him, either way I think they did pretty good in this trade.

And the Knicks have a pretty obvious upside where KAT is somewhat significantly better than Randle in my opinion and he and Jalen Brunson might make a deadly PnR duo.
