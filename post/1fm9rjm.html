The euro-step and the step back jump shot

I’ve never seen anyone equate these two moves, but they’re basically variations on the same footwork, using the off foot to go in an unexpected direction. I thought about this after reading a thread on Kiki Vanderweghe’s use of the step back, which was a rare move in his time, and thinking that Kiki and Manu Ginobili had a similar look when trying to keep defenders off balance with awkward shots.
