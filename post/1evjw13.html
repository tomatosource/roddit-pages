With Guerschon Yabusele signing with the 76ers, Real Madrid has now earned €20.300.000 (around $22.400.000) over the past decade just from NBA buyouts

Source

The seven players Real Madrid received buyouts from NBA teams for are:

  • Facundo Campazzo - €6.000.000 from the Nuggets in 2020

  • Sergio Rodriguez - €3.000.000 from the 76ers in 2016

  • Usman Garuba - €3.000.000 from the Rockets in 2021

  • Nikola Mirotic - €2.500.000 from the Bulls in 2014

  • Guerschon Yabusele - €2.500.000 from the 76ers in 2024

  • Luka Doncic - €2.000.000 from the Mavericks in 2018

  • Gabriel Deck - €1.300.000 from the Thunder in 2021

An interesting fact is that aside from Luka, who still plays in the NBA, and Yabusele, who just signed with thr 76ers, the only player not to return to Real Madrid after his NBA stint was Nikola Mirotic (who signed the biggest contract in European basketball history with Barcelona).
