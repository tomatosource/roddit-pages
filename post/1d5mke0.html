Andrew Wiggins would be ‘honoured’ to play for Canada at Olympics: ‘We’ll see what happens’

Article

The Toronto Star recently interviewed Andrew Wiggins and asked about potentially playing for Canada at the Olympics this summer.

“I’d be honoured to play for our country but we’ll see what happens,” Wiggins said on a Zoom call

“Just stay tuned and we’ll see what the road brings.”

“I love playing,” he said of the two events. ”Playing it was definitely enjoying for myself. It was fun every time.”

Wiggins confirmed that he has been invited to training camp in June.

He will be extended an invitation to a training that begins in late June in Toronto and will be given every chance to get one of what are expected to be three open spots on the roster.

I think he will likely be selected to the team. Whether or not he will be in the starting 5 is a different question.
