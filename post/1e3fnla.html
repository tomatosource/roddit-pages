[Burchardt] Christian Koloko participated in private FA workout in LV on Saturday, per agent Calvin Andrews. Koloko’s paperwork has been submitted so the NBA’s Fitness-to-Play can make a determination on his status.

Source: https://twitter.com/D_Burchardt/status/1812339430847561812

Christian Koloko participated in private FA workout in LV on Saturday, his agent Calvin Andrews of KlutchSports tells The U.S. Sun. Workout also featured Eric Bledsoe, Talen Horton-Tucker, Cory Joseph, Austin Rivers, DJ Wilson, Thon Maker, BJ Boston, and Juan Toscano-Anderson.

Koloko is yet to be medically cleared by the NBA’s Fitness-to-Play panel after suffering a blood clot issue, preventing him from playing for or practicing with an NBA team. But the 7-foot center’s been cleared to return to basketball by his own doctors, per Andrews.

Koloko’s paperwork has been submitted so the NBA’s Fitness-to-Play can make a determination on his status.

Sounds like Christian Koloko may be nearing a return to the NBA; he was recently spotted with the Raptors during their Summer League game where they blew out the OKC Thunder 94-69.
