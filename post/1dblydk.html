There’s only ONE group of 5 players that was named to the All-NBA 1st Team together more than once – Wilt Chamberlain, Elgin Baylor, Bob Pettit, Oscar Robertson, Jerry West – who were all selected for the 1st Team in both 1962 and 1964.

There are, however, several foursomes who’ve been named to the All-NBA 1st Team together in at least 3 different seasons.

  1. Charles Barkley, Karl Malone, Magic Johnson, Michael Jordan - 1989, 1990, 1991
  2. Dwight Howard, Kevin Durant, LeBron James, Kobe Bryant - 2010, 2011, 2012
  3. Elgin Baylor, Bob Pettit, Oscar Robertson, Jerry West - 1962, 1963, 1964

The fifth players in those stretches:

  1. Olajuwon (89), Ewing (90), Robinson (91)
  2. Wade (10), Rose (11), Paul (12)
  3. Chamberlain (62), Russell (63), Chamberlain (64)
