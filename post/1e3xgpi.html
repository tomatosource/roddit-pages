Rockets assistant coach and former Spurs center Tiago Splitter will be the new head coach of Euroleague newcomer Paris, replacing the Grizzlies’ new assistant coach Tuomas Iisalo at that position

Source

Splitter spent the last season as the assistant coach of the Rockets, after spending four seasons being an assistant with the Nets.

Splitter’s also the assistant coach of the Brazilian national team, who just qualified for the Paris Olympics by winning the Olympic Qualifying Tournament in Riga. He’s been at that position for the last three years, and has also coached the Brazilian U23 national team to the gold at the GLOBL Jam Tournament in Toronto in 2022.

He’ll be replacing Tuomas Iisalo on the Paris bench, given that the Finnish expert signed for the Grizzlies to be a top assistant coach. Iisalo won the Eurocup with the French team the past season, leading them to their first ever season in the Euroleague.
