Non Superstars that you think will be superstars when they reach their prime

Superstars (Top 5-7 players in the league) are usually in their peak ages (26-34 yrs old). Like Joker, Luka, Giannis, Embiid, SGA, AD, Tatum, etc.

But who are the players who are not superstars yet. But you think will be a Superstar MVP Candidate level player when he reach his prime ages (26-34 yrs old)?

This will be mine:

Wemby- Yes, I think Wemby’s hype is legit. Barring injury, I think the worst for him is a perrenial MVP Candidate. But the ceiling can be as high as Top 10 all time player. (Maybe more. Some people arguing his ceiling is GOAT. But I am not going that far yet). But for sure, barring any injuries, he will be a surefire superstar.

Edwards- I think many people are not realizing that Ant having in the same discussion with Top SGs in their prime Booker and Mitchell when Ant is like 4 full years younger is a feat of itself. Heck, some people even argue Ant is already better than Book/Spida. And Ant is nowhere near his prime yet. Booker and Mitchell are in the middle of their absolute primes. Yet, Pre Prime Ant is in the same realm as them. Imagine how good Ant will be when he is as old as Booker and Mitchell now.

Paolo- Yes, I am buying on Paolo’s Potential. That build is amazing. A Potential 6’10 Point Forward is very rare these days. Especially with All Nba level talent. And Paolo is the youngest in this list. He is just 21. He will be scary at 26-34 yrs old. Paolo’s talent in conjuction and in correlation to his physical build is just too great to ignore.

Hot Take: I think Paolo will be better than Ja and Zion when all are in their primes.
