Bruno Caboclo had a private workout in front of 17 teams in Las Vegas

Source

Caboclo flew to Las Vegas, where the Summer League is being held, yesterday and had a private workout with 17 teams.

After performing great at the last World Cup (16 PTS, 9 REB, 1 BLK averages on 57/31/81 shooting splits) with Brazil, Caboclo signed for Italian club Reyer Venezia, for which he didn’t play a single game for because his agent and him found a loophole for him to terminate the contract by himself, without the consent of the club, so that he can sign in the Euroleague with Partizan Belgrade. The Italian club took it to the courts to appeal the decision, and won in the Italian courts, but the Euroleague allowed the termination of his contract.

He spent the past season playing for Partizan Belgrade in the Euroleague where he averaged 9 PTS and 4 REB on 60/50/73 shooting splits.

Before the Serbian league finals series Bruno Caboclo flew to Florida, without informing anyone at the club, because of a custody hearing regarding his child. He was punished with five monthly salaries for breaking club protocol in leaving without announcing it during the season.

Caboclo’s agent tried to terminate his contract with Partizan as well, but the Serbian club wouldn’t allow it and are looking to let Caboclo go with a buyout fee, since he has a year left on his contract.

Caboclo was the main reason Brazil won the Olympic Qualifying Tournament in Riga and qualified for the Paris Olympics, averaging 18 PTS, 7 REB and 2 BLK on 67/57/91 shooting splits during the tournament.

He already spent seven seasons in the NBA, playing with the Raptors, the Kings, the Grizzlies and the Rockets.
