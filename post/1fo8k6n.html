MVP finals winner gets a car

I just found out that the NBA Finals MVP used to get a car. Back in 1979, Dennis Johnson took home the MVP title and a Volkswagen Scirocco when the Sonics won the NBA Championship.

I saw Wes Unseld got a car in 1978 but couldnt see Magic getting one in 1980. The car was provided by Sport Magazine. Does anyone know when this stopped? I wonder what the car would be today if this still continued?

Edit: I did a bit more research Sport Magazine started giving out prizes for the finals MVP when the award started in 1969. Dennis Johnson was the last person to get a car, it was a gold watch and a $5,000 scholarship to the college of the players choice.
