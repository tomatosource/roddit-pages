What is your favorite one-hit wonder season/player?

I love the flash in the pan players who have a great year followed by a regression to the mean for the following years. Or a team that overachieves for that one season and has everyone anticipating a great second year only to be let down. I just like that phenomenon.

Historically, I look at Michael Adams who had an incredible statistical year in like 1990 followed by let downs the following years. Dale Ellis was another player who was a superstar for a year or two and then just was an average player following. Dana Barros had that amazing year… Kevin Gamble had a good playoff run for the celtics and then couldn’t replicate it with increased minutes during the regular season. As far as teams go, I remember the Bullets had a good set of core players during the 1996-97 season and just succumbed to lackadasical play and injuries.
