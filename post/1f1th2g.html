[Mod Post] New Mod Application Thread

Hi all!

It’s been a while since we brought on new mods, and a new season is just around the corner. With recent changes, some of our older mods have moved on, and we’re looking to bring in some more mods that are interested in improving this sub.

What does a mod do?

Review posts/comments/messages, update sidebar pictures, respond to mod mail, and potential botting work, as well as other miscellaneous stuff that comes up.

How much work is it?

We are a very active sub, with a lot of users, so there’s usually stuff. We don’t have any set amount of time you need to put in, but ideally if you’re on the sub a lot it just becomes part of the flow.

Why would I want to mod?

Simply, you want to make the sub a better place to discuss basketball. There’s no pay, there’s not much thanks, so basically only if you want the subreddit to be better.

What do I need in order to mod?

A computer, a reddit account with 2FA active, and discord to partake in modteam communication.

#APPLICATION

  • What time zone are you in?

  • Roughly how often do you browse r/nba?

  • How long have you been a part of the community and why do you think you’d be a good addition to the mod team?

  • Is there anything you’d like to change about the subreddit?

  • What is your favorite NBA memory?

  • Do you have any skills that you feel would benefit our subreddit mod team (teamwork, coding, graphic design, etc.)? *this can be nothing

The rest is up to you guys. Feel free to add anything else that you think might help make a case for why you should be a moderator for /r/nba.

All unrelated comments will be removed for sake of readability. Keep in mind that the amount of upvotes you get in this thread will not have an impact on your application. Please direct all questions to the sticky comment.

Thank you as always!
