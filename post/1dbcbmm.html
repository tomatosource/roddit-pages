Is Al Horford a hall of famer?

His resume is as follows:

5× NBA All-Star (2010, 2011, 2015, 2016, 2018)

All-NBA Third Team (2011)

NBA All-Defensive Second Team (2018)

NBA All-Rookie First Team (2008)

2× NCAA champion (2006, 2007)

Third-team All-American – AP (2007)

SEC tournament MVP (2007)

2008 Centrobasket bronze medal

2011 FIBA Americas bronze medal

2012 Centrobasket gold medal

He has just 14167 total NBA career points and 8574 total rebounds, but he had tremendous success in the collegiate world and some success with his national team. I think he is a HOF given his collegiate accomplishments and overall successful NBA career. 2 national championships in college is massive deal. And IMO a championship here would solidify his chances and cement his resume as a winner at every single level. Thoughts?

Remember, it’s the basketball hall of fame, not just the NBA HOF
