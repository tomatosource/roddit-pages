If the NBA were to add 3v3 to the all-star weekend where every team competed, what team would win and with who?

I’m going Mavs with Kyrie, Luka, and Lively. Here’s why:

  • Kyrie Irving:

    • Exceptional ball-handling and creativity.
    • Clutch performer with a history of making big shots.
    • Strong one-on-one offensive skills.

  • Luka Dončić:

    • Versatile playmaker with excellent vision and passing.
    • High basketball IQ and ability to control the game tempo.
    • Can score from anywhere on the court.

  • Dereck Lively II:

    • Athletic and strong defensive presence.
    • Excellent shot-blocking and rebounding abilities.
    • Provides physicality and rim protection.

Combination Benefits:

  • Offensive Versatility: Kyrie and Luka can create their own shots and play off each other, keeping defenses off balance.
  • Defensive Balance: Lively provides the necessary interior defense and rebounding, complementing Kyrie and Luka’s perimeter defense.
  • Playmaking and Scoring: Luka’s playmaking combined with Kyrie’s scoring ability makes them hard to defend.

This trio combines elite offense, strong defense, and high basketball IQ, making them formidable in a 3v3 format.
