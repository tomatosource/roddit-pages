The last 6 NBA Champions are all different teams. That’s only happened one other time in NBA history

The last 6 champions are:

2019: Raptors

2020: Lakers

2021: Bucks

2022: Warriors

2023: Nuggets

2024: Celtics

I could definitely be wrong, but the only other instance I found of this level of championship parity was 1975-1980:

1975: Warriors

1976: Celtics

1977: Blazers

1978: Bullets

1979: Supersonics

1980: Lakers

Just thought it was neat that we’re currently in the least dynastic era in NBA history.
