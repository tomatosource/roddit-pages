So assuming that Vegas and Seattle are getting expansion teams after the TV deal is finalized, who is moving to the East to balance the conferences?

The worst kept secret for years has been that Seattle and Las Vegas are next in line for expansion teams. There were rumors of it happening soonish around 2019, but the pandemic put a halt to that. Adam Silver has said that the league wanted to get a new TV deal done before talking about expansion. Well here we are. It is time to bring back the Supersonics.

So when that happens, the league will have 15 teams in the East and 17 in the West. The simple solution is to move either Memphis or Minnesota to the East. Memphis is slightly east of Minneapolis, but the Grizzlies are geographically closer to some divisional rivals. The T-Wolves are farther away from most of the conference. Putting them in the same division as the Bucks and Bulls could be beneficial.
