What team are you a believer in for either this upcoming season or for their core long-term?

Last week, I posted in here asking if there was a team that you didn’t see the vision for, aka what team who you don’t think is going to be very good this year or who’s long term plan makes no sense.

Now, I wanted to shift the conversation in a more positive direction and ask what’s the one team that you believe in more than most? Now, this isn’t like a “who’s the best team” or anything like that, rather it’s a team who you think is going to be much better than what seems to be the consensus. This can either be a team that you think is going to overperform their expectations this year, or a team who’s not competing this year but is on the right track and could be relevant again in just a couple of years.

For me, it’s the Atlanta Hawks. Their roster is super young (Trae could be their oldest starter) and has a lot of room for growth. I think Dyson Daniels is much better of a fit next to Trae since he isn’t very ball dominant and a good defender (essentially what people thought DJM would be) and a Risaccher/Jalen Johnson wing combo has a lot of potential to be really good and really fun. Another thing is that they don’t own their picks for the next 3 years, so there’s literally no reason for them to head towards a rebuild unless they get their picks back from San Antonio. With no incentive to tank, I think they’ll try and add at the deadline or next FA cycle to try and become a real threat in the east
