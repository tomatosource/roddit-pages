Ish Smith is still unsigned. Is this the end for the record-holding journeyman?

Ish Smith holds the record of 13 different NBA teams that he has played for in his 14 year career. He played last year for his hometown Charlotte Hornets, a team he already played for in the 2022 season. The next player in line is Garrett Temple who has played for 12 teams in 14 years. He is still signed to Toronto so it is unlikely he will tie Smith unless waived or traded. I doubt this due to Temple’s value as a leader for a young Toronto team. There are 4 other players that played for 12 teams but have since retired. I believe Jeff Green is the next active player with 11 teams in 16 seasons. He is still signed to Houston who he already played for in 2020.

Charlotte fans, how was he last season? He’s 36 now, not as fast as he used to be and looking at the end of his career. Do you guys think it’s likely over? Let me know your thoughts!

I hope Smith finds a team for this season. He’s a solid veteran with championship experience that can help boost a bench with a Udonis Haslem/Garrett Temple-type role. What team could use him? 14 teams played for would be a pretty solid record that could hold for a long time.
