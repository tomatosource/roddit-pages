Who first used a name suffix (Jr.,Sr.) on his jersey?

I am wondering who was the first NBA player to put his name suffix on his jersey? By name suffix, I mean Sr., Jr., II, III, and so on.

I looked at basketball-reference to search for players with name suffixes, and I list here those who came to the NBA earliest, per suffix:

Jr.

  • Tim Hardaway Jr. : 2013-14
  • Otto Porter Jr. : 2013-14
  • Glen Rice Jr. : 2013-14

Sr.

  • Xavier Tillman Sr. : 2020-21

II

  • Larry Drew II : 2014-15
  • Gary Payton II : 2016-17

III

  • John Lucas III : 2005-06
  • James Ennis III : 2014-15
  • Glenn Robinson III : 2014-15

IV

  • Lonnie Walker IV : 2018-19
  • McKinley Wright IV : 2021-22

John Lucas III used only Lucas in his jersey early in his career up to at least the 2013-14 season:

  • https://www.gettyimages.ca/detail/news-photo/jacque-vaughn-of-the-san-antonio-spurs-looks-to-move-the-news-photo/72937232
  • https://www.gettyimages.ca/detail/news-photo/damian-lillard-of-the-portland-trail-blazers-passes-against-news-photo/184810348

Tim Hardaway Jr. has been using Jr. in his jersey as early as 2013-14:

  • https://www.gettyimages.ca/detail/news-photo/jimmy-butler-of-the-chicago-bulls-looks-to-pass-over-tim-news-photo/187146640

Otto Porter Jr. used only Porter during 2013-14:

  • https://www.gettyimages.ca/detail/news-photo/the-back-of-otto-porter-jr-22-of-the-washington-wizards-news-photo/462669805

Glen Rice Jr. used only Rice during 2013-14:

  • https://www.gettyimages.ca/detail/news-photo/chris-paul-of-the-los-angeles-clippers-shoots-against-glen-news-photo/456616487

Therefore, Tim Hardaway Jr. is the first NBA player to put his name suffix on his NBA jersey. Or did I miss someone?
