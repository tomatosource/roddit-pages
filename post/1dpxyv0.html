In 2005, the Magic drafted Fran Vázquez in the lottery at #11. He never played in the NBA.

https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/2005_NBA_draft

As of 2023, he is the only player since the merger to be drafted in the lottery (or top 14 before the current lottery system) who never played in the NBA (other than Len Bias in ‘86, who… yeah). Do Magic fans remember this? What was the story there? It sounds like he just changed his mind about playing in the NBA or something. A dude getting drafted in the lottery and then never touching the floor for an NBA team is wild, I don’t think you’d ever see that today (barring some kind of freak injury)

Also, fun fact, the next highest drafted player to never play in the NBA (again since the merger, we’re not counting ABA guys here) was Frédéric Weis at #15, who is best remembered as “that big French dude Vince Carter dunked on that one time”
