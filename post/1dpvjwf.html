If the Mavericks decide to keep the 58th draft pick, it will be the first time since 2018 that the Milwaukee Bucks did not possess, make or trade for the last pick of the draft

2023 - Bucks take Chris Livingston with the last pick of the draft (their own pick)

2022 - Pacers take Hugo Besson and immediately trade rights to the Bucks

2021 - Pacers trade pick 60 to the bucks as part of a trade down to 31, select Georgios Kalaitzakis

2020 - Pelicans pick Sam Merrill who was immediately sent to Milwaukee as part of the Jrue Holiday trade

2019 - 60th pick was the bucks pick but had been traded to the Kings in 2013

Hilariously, the last pick not associated with the bucks in 2018 was Kostas Antetokounmpo
