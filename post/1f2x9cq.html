Who do you think is the bigger threat to the Boston Celtics going into the 2024⁄25 NBA season: Milwaukee Bucks or New York Knicks

Self-explanatory topic on this post. The Milwaukee Bucks replaced Beverley, Crowder, Gallinari, Beasley, with Gary Trent Jr., Taurean Prince, Delon Wright. They’ve significantly improved their depth around their stars. Giannis goes into the season healthy and rested. Middleton had surgery on both ankles during the offseason, which was described as clean up surgery for both, and he should be ready for camp, though they will surely take things slowly with Middleton. He has missed time and had some significant injuries in recent years but even with the surgeries should be going into this season the healthiest he has been in a while. Dame says he feels great and he should go into the season in much better shape than last year with all the drama that swirled around him.

The Knicks added OG Anunoby during the season last winter and added Mikal Bridges during the offseason, trading five first round picks and a swap for his services, but nobody meaningful from the current roster. Julius Randle is recovering well from his shoulder surgery and Mitchell Robinson is continuing to rehab his ankle after surgery to repair a stress fracture. Julius seems on track to start the season but Mitch may not. Knicks have the Nova connection and are a team with excellent chemistry and coaching. People seem to view them as a defensive team first, but they’ve been a top 5 offense in the NBA the last two seasons with Brunson and Randle healthy, and now have added an elite third banana in Mikal.

They were the 6th and 7th ranked offenses respectively last season and there is a very fair argument to be made they both end up top 3 this year. Defensively, Bucks have Giannis/Brook who remain an elite pair. Knicks have OG/Mikal, which should be an elite wing pair. When Mitch is healthy, he’s a positive on D, but much less so when not and they’re thin behind him. Both teams have some potential vulnerabilities on defense.

Which team do you consider the bigger threat to Boston going into the season?
