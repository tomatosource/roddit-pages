This Finals series has the biggest discrepancy between what the experts (Vegas) think and everyone else (fans, media)

  • Vegas has the Celtics as significant favorites to defeat the Mavericks in these Finals with implied odds of 66%. (Source: FanDuel)

Now, some people might say “oh that doesn’t mean anything, Vegas is just trying to balanc-” nope. Common misconception. Vegas in the modern era is trying to predict things accurately. And 89% of all bets are on the Mavs to win the Finals. Yet the lines haven’t moved, because Vegas is content with what they set it at.

Contrast this with:

  • Nine ESPN analysts picked the Mavericks to win the Finals compared to eight for the Celtics. Source

  • Most online polls on the Finals have been won by the Mavericks significantly (can’t find an example rn but if anyone’s on Twitter they’ve probably seen them)

  • The average fan likes the Mavericks chances more, usually citing Kyrie/Luka and just showing an overall lack of belief in Tatum and the Celtics (and not really valuing their overall rotation that much because starpower is what moves people).

I’m sure a lot of people are gonna kinda cope and say “oh everyone knows the Celtics are winning but they just want the Mavs to win”. Yes, they want they want the Mavs to win, but it’s clear that a lot of people think the Mavs are better. I’ve been an NBA fan for years, but I’ve never seen this level of discrepancy between Vegas/the public in a Finals series.

And to be clear I’m not some Celtics fan arguing that they’re the underdogs. I think Vegas is right, and in the future we will rightfully view the Celtics as having been significant favorites this series. It doesn’t really matter what fans/media think. It’s just funny.
