Former Yuta, NBA player, suffered a torn left calf muscle, questionable for Olympics.

Yuta Watanabe revealed he suffered a torn left calf muscle in early June and aggravated it later that month. When originally diagnosed, he was told that it could sideline him for as long as 8 weeks but he re-injured it and made it worse during the rehab process.

He said “There is not much time left until the games, and it’s up in the air whether the injury will be healed in time.”
