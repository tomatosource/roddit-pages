We’re currently in LeBron’s longest Finals drought of his career.

LeBron debuted in the 2003-2004 season and he led the Cavs to the Finals in his 4th season. He would make the Finals again in his 8th season and played until June every year until 2018 (his 15th season) with both the Heat and Cavs. He went to the Finals in his 17th season with the Lakers.

&nbsp;

Before late April 2024 when the Lakers got bounced out of the playoffs, LeBron’s longest Finals drought was only 3 seasons! It also marked the first time in a decade in which he didn’t make the Finals for at least half of his career. (10 Finals appearances in now 21 seasons)
