Olympic 3v3 is kind of wack

This is my first time watching. I don’t know what I expected, but it definitely wasn’t this.

There’s a 12-second shot clock, and the clock starts ticking as soon as the last team’s basket is made. So they have to grab the ball out of the net after a make, and then sprint behind the 3-pt line (or pass) before they can start their offense. And if they opt to pass the ball after a made basket, the defense can steal the ball.

Seriously?? No one in the real world plays 3v3 like this. I can understand wanting to speed the game up for the sake of entertainment. But the ultra-short shot clock and weird rule of not checking it up at the top of the key after a made basket makes it feel so gimmicky. It’s no wonder our best player who agreed to participate is Jimmer Fredette.

Don’t get me started on how there’s free throws too lol.
