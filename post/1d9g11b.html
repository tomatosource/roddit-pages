EuroBasket champions Spain announce 22-man roster for the Olympic Qualifying Tournament in Valencia, former World Cup MVP Ricky Rubio not on the list

Source

Santi Aldama (Grizzlies) and Usman Garuba (Warriors) are the only NBA players on the roster, which also includes All-Euroleague selection Lorenzo Brown (Maccabi Tel Aviv), the reigning EuroBasket MVP Willy Hernangomez (Barcelona), Euroleague champion Juancho Hernangomez (Panathinaikos), and Spanish legends Rudy Fernandez and Sergio Llull (Real Madrid), among others.

Spain is the host of their Olympic Qualifying Tournament, scheduled to take place at the start of July, and will compete against Lebanon and Angola in group play.

The top two teams from that group match up against the group which consists of Finland, Poland and the Bahamas, in a single-elimination semi-final and final knockout phase, and the winner of the tournament earns a place at the Paris Olympics.
