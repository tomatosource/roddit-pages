UNIFORM NEWS: Philadelphia 76ers tease return to Black AI-era uniforms in new Tyrese Maxey video and corresponding Slam cover!

The Philadelphia 76ers have teased a return to their AI-era Black uniforms that they wore in the 2001 NBA Finals. In a video commemorating Tyrese Maxey’s rise to stardom, Philly gave Maxey his own custom black jersey, as well as presenting him with a Slam cover that says “The fans asked and Philly answered; 76ers classic edition”.

One thing to note is that they are using the official lingo of Nike-speak when it comes to uniforms. Just as home and away uniforms have the designations of “association edition” and “icon edition”, and alternates have “statement edition” and “city edition”, any throwback uniforms used in Nike’s program have the designation “classic edition”. We’ll see what it means….
