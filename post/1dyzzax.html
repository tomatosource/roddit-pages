Hachimura and Watanabe spearhead Japan’s final 12-man roster for the Paris Olympics

Source

Lakers power forward Rui Hachimura and former Grizzlies forward Yuta Watanabe spearhead Japan’s final 12-man roster for the Paris Olympics.

Other big names on the roster include last year’s World Cup standout Josh Hawkinson, Pacers new signing Keisei Tominaga and Japan’s league last year’s MVP Yuki Kawamura.

Makoro Hiejima, Yuki Togashi, Yudai Baba, Akira Jacobs, Kai Toews, Hugh Watanabe and Hirotaka Yoshii complete the roster.

Japan will play in the same group as France, Germany and Brazil at the Paris Olympics.
