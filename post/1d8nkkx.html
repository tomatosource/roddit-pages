3 different NBA assistant coaches predict Celtics vs Mavericks: “Going to pick Boston with or without Kristaps Porzingis”

Source

Sportskeeda spoke to 3 NBA assistant coaches from 3 different playoff teams, all of whom were granted anonymity to speak candidly about the Mavericks and Celtics, which play Game 1 on Thursday (8:30 pm ET, ABC). They analyzed the series, discussed the ramifications of Porzingis’ availability and detailed the chemistry between Doncic and Irving.
