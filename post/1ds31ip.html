Notes:

  • The 2020 Finals were the only Finals in this whole list where both teams had missed the playoffs the year before.

  • The 2023 Finals are the only Finals since 2019-20 to feature two teams that made the playoffs the previous year.

  • On top of this, none of the last 5 defending champs have reached the conference finals.

  • Since 2019-20, only 3 out of 20 teams that were coming off a conference finals berth made it back to the conference finals (nobody in 2020, 2021 and 2022, Heat and Celtics in 2023, Celtics in 2024).

This paints a picture of league that has changed massively since the KD-Warriors broke up. Since then we have had an unprecedented likelihood of teams going from bad to contenders in a single year, and contenders falling off in a single year.

I think this is important to keep in mind when looking at how quickly fans are dismiss the chances of a deep playoff run of any team that missed the playoffs / lost in round 1 the previous year, and to stop pencilling-in every team that looked like a contender the previous year to be one again the next year.
