2020-2024: The USA vs The World Era

In NBA2k they’ve labeled certain periods as eras Such as the Jordan Era, Kobe era, Magic vs Bird era, etc

If I were to call this era something (2020-2024) I would say the USA vs World Era

The past five NBA champions have been alternating led by the U.S. then a world player (Lebron [U.S.] then Giannis [World] then Curry [U.S.] then Jokic [World] then Tatum/Brown [U.S.])

  • There’s been some discourse over foreign players in the league recently, especially with podcasts and such
  • The past 5 MVPs have been from outside the U.S. (if you count Embiid as a foreign player if not then 4 out of 5)
  • Some notable rivalries today are headlined by a U.S. and foreign player example Booker vs Doncic or Lebron vs Jokic -And it’s not technically NBA but the Olympics were like a big finale to all this

Any thoughts?
