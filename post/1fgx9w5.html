[OC] A Look at The Best Player on Every Championship Team

A large portion of NBA talk is rings, whether it be to herald a player or to diminish them. Many enjoy pulling out the “Well, is Robert Horry better than X?”, to which the rebuttal is inevitably “It matters for the best, not role-players”. Despite this acknowledgement, and all the conversations surrounding so-called “bus-drivers”, there hasn’t been much discussion on who we view these players to be historically.

Here’s my take on the best player for each league champion since 1946, taking both regular season and playoffs into account. Obviously, the earlier the season, the more nebulous the take, but I tried to research enough to come to a conclusion.

For some years, the best player was obvious and inarguable. However, a higher number than I expected were more debateable, to varying degrees. The contenders column isn’t a examination of the margins of these cases. Some were very close, other were decisive (despite the potential for someone else having an argument). I’m not going to get into all of that. I have simply boldened who, in my opinion, was the better player that season. Let me know where you agree or disagree.
