The insanely poor 3 Point shooting in the NBA Finals this year

  • Maxi Kleber: 16%
  • Kristaps Porzingis: 22%
  • Jaylen Brown: 23%
  • Luka Doncic: 24%
  • Derrick Jones Jr: 25%
  • Jayson Tatum: 26%
  • PJ Washington: 27%
  • Kyrie Irving: 27%

Everyone keeps talking about how bad Tatum shot in the Finals and saying this is the reason he lost Finals MVP. Tatum didn’t get many layups or open shots, because he lead the entire Finals in assists as he was frequently doubled and used to create gravity or space.

But either way, everyone important shot bad. The top 5 players in the series all shot under 30% from 3.
