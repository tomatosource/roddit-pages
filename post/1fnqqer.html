Let’s go over the Raptors success under Vince Carter:

1998⁄99 - Missed the playoffs; Vince wins RotY

99⁄00 - Made the playoffs, lost to the Knicks in a sweep

00/01 - Made the playoffs, won vs the Knicks in 5, lost to the 76ers in a controversial Game 7

02/03 - Missed the playoffs; Vince suggested that the recently drafted Bosh should be traded

03/04 - Missed the playoffs

04/05 - Vince did not finish the season as a Raptor to see them miss the playoffs, as he was traded in December (Among other events, he assaulted his coach during the leadup to the Nets trade)

None of Vince’s seasons with Toronto managed to crack 50 wins in one of the weakest eras for the Eastern Conference.
