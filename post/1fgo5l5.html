The all-time ‘K’ team is STACKED

I just realised how insanely stacked a team featuring only players whose first name starts with the letter ‘K’ is:

G: Kobe Bryant

G: Kawhi Leonard

F: Kevin Durant

F: Kevin Garnett

F: Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

Bench:

Kevin McHale

Karl Malone

Klay Thompson

Kyrie Irving

Kevin Love

Kyle Lowry

Kevin Johnson

KC Jones

I limited it to a 13-man roster which is why I stopped here, otherwise other notable players include - Kristaps Porzingis, Karl Anthony-Towns, Kevin Willis, Kelly Tripucka, Kenyon Martin, Kevin Loughery, Kiki Vandeweghe & Khris Middleton.

If you want to include players whose surname begins with ‘K’, we add:

Jason Kidd, Bernard King, Shawn Kemp, Toni Kukoc, Steve Kerr & Kyle Korver.

‘K’ is probably the winner in the all-time alphabet contest, so who do you think comes 2nd?

Notable contenders include:

‘L’: Larry Bird, LeBron James, Luka Doncic, LaMarcus Aldridge, Lew Alcindor, Lenny Wilkens, Louie Dampier, Lou Hudson

’M’: Michael Jordan, Magic Johnson, Moses Malone, Marc Gasol, Manu Ginobili, Maurice Stokes, Mark Aguirre, Mitch Richmond

’S’: Shaquille O’Neal, Steve Nash, Sam Jones, Scottie Pippen, Steph Curry, Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, Spencer Haywood, Sydney Moncrief

‘J’: Jerry West, Jimmy Butler, Joel Embiid, John Havlicek, James Harden, Julius Erving, Jayson Tatum, John Stockton

’D’: Dirk Nowitzki, Devin Booker, David Robinson, Damian Lillard, Draymond Green, Dywane Wade, Dominique Wilkins, Dwight Howard

‘B’: Bill Russell, Bob Cousy, Bill Walton, Bob Pettit, Bernard King, Ben Wallace, Blake Griffin, Bobby Jones

‘P’: Patrick Ewing, Pau Gasol, Paul Arizin, Paul George, Pete Maravich, Peja Stojakovic, Penny Hardaway, Paul Pierce

Which other alphabet can you think of with such a stacked all-time team?

(Note: This is limited to NBA careers only, hence the omission of Mel Daniels)
