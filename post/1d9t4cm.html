The 76ers offense w/out Joel Embiid vs the Knicks was downright disgusting

News came out a couple days ago that Philly could look at signing Joel Embiid to a 3 yr/$193.5mil extension this summer, and there was a lot of debate in the comments under the post here about if he’s worth the money or not. Personally, I think it’s a no brainer to keep Embiid at all costs despite him being perpetually injured, and here’s why.

First off, Philly was just 16-27 when Embiid didn’t play this year, a definitive lottery team in the already lackluster Eastern conference. With him, they were 31-8, on pace for a similar record as the Celtics, but the disparity in the playoffs between the Embiid and Embiid-less Sixers might’ve been even worse in the playoffs.

Embiid sat for 44 minutes, 44 seconds in Philly’s first round series. In that time, Philly was outscored a whopping 111-64. They shot just 25-83 from the field, a putrid 30% (47% with Embiid on the court, and just 7-32 from 3, 22% (40% with Embiid on the court). This is especially damning considering just how close the series was, their 4 losses were only by a combined 18 points. Imagine if their offense didn’t just turn into a headless chicken the second Embiid left the floor…

Oh yeah, Embiid was also doing this on one leg and half a face, in case people forgot. I get that he’s always hurt, and it’s always at the most inopportune times, but I don’t think that any team needs their star player more than Philly needs Embiid.
