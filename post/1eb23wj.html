Over/Under: 25 wins for the Detroit Pistons

I predicted on the Pistons board and elsewhere a few weeks ago the Pistons could win up to 32 games, while likely being around 30 when all is said and done, but now looking at their roster and the rest of the league honestly and just taking everything into account, I can’t go that far with the Pistons. Someone has to lose, and they’re still the worst or second worst roster in the association.

That being said, they do have a roster that makes some sense, you can expect internal growth from their youngsters, Cade is able to have a normal offseason for a change vs rehabbing, and they do have a coach who will be responsible for his team on the floor and demand effort. I think Bickerstaff is a better coach than he gets credit for but is still a below average one in relation to the league but that’s all relative and subjective.

I personally don’t see the Pistons winning more than 27. 27-55 would be a huge step forward. Pistons aren’t tanking this season unless things really get ugly or they are in position for #1. They’ll tank if it gets there but otherwise they wanna play this season out because they wanna identify a core that hopefully a top pick next season can develop and grow with. They’re still in talent collection mode and are at least 2-3 years away from playoffs realistically.

I set the Over/Under number at 25. I think that’s a fair number for the Pistons. I easily could see them hit the under, and be around 18-22 wins. But I could also easily see them match or slightly surpass 25. It seems like many on here and the web expect more from the Pistons than low-20s wins so I set the number at 25 to see what you all think. Over/Under 25 wins for the Pistons this season?
