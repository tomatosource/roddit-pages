The Indiana Pacers are the first team to score 10,000+ points in the regular season since the 1983-84 Nuggets

The 2023-2024 Pacers scored 10,110 points in 82 regular season games. Tyrese & Myles accounted for over a quarter of those points with 1300+ each and another 8 players scored 600+ points.

This hasn’t been done since 1984 Nuggets who scored 10147 points. Led by Alex English & Kiki Vandeweghe who scored 2167 & 2295 respectively. Dan Issel added on another 1504 points & only 3 other players on the team scored 600+ points.
