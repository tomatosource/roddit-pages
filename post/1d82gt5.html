Hypothetical Yugoslavian National Team for 2024 Paris Olympics

Watching the ESPN documentary ‘Once Brothers’, on the frailed relationship of Drazen Petrovic and Vlade Divac, and their triumph and ultimate demise of the Yugoslavian National Team. I thought how amazing it would be if Yugoslavia still existed in a world in which the top 2 players in the World are from the Slavic bloc of nations. So as a fun exercise I tried piecing together what the roster would look like if they were to compete in the Paris Olympics this summer.
