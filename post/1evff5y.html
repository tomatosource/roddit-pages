Is the upcoming 2024⁄25 NBA season the best situation Joel Embiid has had surrounding him in Philadelphia?

I say best situation and not just roster when including coaching and team chemistry.

With Butler and Prime Harris you could argue that Sixers squad had more top end talent but when you look at the overall rotation, I think it can be said this is the best team Embiid will have ever played with. PG is their third option.

I’m a bit skeptical of Oubre myself, but can’t argue he had some of the best basketball in a winning environment of his career last season. Yabusele’s translation back to NBA ball will be a question, but from his International team play to the Olympics and play for France, the things he’s done well are things he’s doing consistently in all environments and important skills for what the Sixers need at that 4 spot.

They have depth, they have shooting, they have size, and they have more than two guys who can create shots for themselves. On paper, I think this is the best team Embiid has ever had to play with. What do you all think?
