Which NBA game made you a fan?

Yao Ming’s first game against Shaq. Seeing a 7’6 center that’s actually skilled going up against a slightly shorter but physically dominant player like Shaq was so cool to watch. O’Neal won the statistical battle but Yao held his own and Houston won the game. He blocked Shaq’s first 3 shots after all. He became my favorite player and inspired me to play basketball.
