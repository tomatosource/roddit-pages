2024-25 NBA City Edition jerseys have been leaked

Source is this Twitter account

Obviously these are not confirmed, but the images look official and it’s fun to see. Fans of the team can point out any specific references on their jerseys.

Tweet 1: Bulls, Rockets, Celtics, and Pelicans

Bulls: White with eggshell/cream accents, design based off the Chicago flag on the side, “BULLS” in gray across the chest

Rockets: Nearly all white with red and black striping, “H-TOWN” across the chest

Celtics: Black with dark green sleeves and lime green stripe on the sides, “CELTICS” across the chest in lime green

Pelicans: Dark blue with a bone pattern and green sleeves “NOLA” across the chest in gold with green outline

Tweet 2: Nets, Knicks, Jazz, and Spurs

Nets: Black and gray with “BROOKLYN” outlined in red, green, and blue across the chest. Another collaboration with Kaws, who did last years’ city edition

Knicks: White version of last years’ city edition

Jazz: Previously revealed, purple with a mountain design

San Antonio: A blue version of last years’ city edition

Tweet 3: Grizzlies, Mavericks, Raptors, and Heat

Grizzlies: Red with blue and white cursive text with “Memphis” across the chest, Memphis will also feature a 30th anniversary throwback uniform

Mavericks: Similar to an all-white version of last years’ city editions, with light gray text

Raptors: Last years’ city editions in black, with the dunking Raptor wearing Vince Carter’s jersey logo, Toronto will also feature a 30th anniversary throwback uniform

Heat: Red version of last years’ city edition

Tweet 4: 76ers, Hawks, Thunder, and Timberwolves

76ers: A white version of their 2021-22 city editions

Hawks: Like if you took the elements from last years’ city edition and put them on the Dominique Wilkins era jerseys

Thunder: Seemingly based off last years’ city edition, but with the design as side paneling and the front all-black (Bad description)

Timberwolves: White version of last years’ city editions

Tweet 5: Magic, Pacers, Pistons, and Nuggets

Magic: A white version of last years’ city edition

Pacers: A white version of last years’ city edition

Pistons: Eggshell/Cream color with orange siding

Nuggets: White with a mountain design in rainbow lines on the side, “5280” across the front with the same mountain design inside the numbers

Tweet 6: Cavaliers, Clippers, Suns, and Warriors

Cavaliers: Blue with “The Land” across the chest

Clippers: Bright blue version of last years’ city editions

Suns: Purple with “The Valley” across the chest and an orange star, orange design on the siding too

Warriors: Dark blue with “Golden State” in a circular design across the chest, red bars on the siding

Tweet 7: Wizards, Kings (Throwbacks), Bucks, and Lakers

Wizards: Blue version of last years’ city editions

Kings: Throwbacks that were revealed yesterday

Bucks: Blue with “BUCKS” across the chest and cream and black lines

Lakers: Purple to Black gradient with “LakeShow” in yellow across the chest

Tweet 8: Kings (Actual City Edition), Hornets, and Trail Blazers

Kings: Actual City Edition, blue with white and red siding, “KINGS” in white with a red outline across the chest

Hornets: Mint with black and gold stripes, “CHARLOTTE” in black with a gold outline across the chest

Trail Blazers: Black with a square pattern, “rip city” logo in white with a red outline across the chest
