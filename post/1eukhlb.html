Nikola Jokic, Luka Doncic, Dirk Nowitzki and Steve Nash anchor the star-studded lineup for Goran Dragic’s farewell game, numerous other current/former NBA players and coaches will participate as well

Source

Current NBA players Nikola Jokic, Luka Doncic, Bogdan Bogdanovic, Nikola Vucevic, Boban Marjanovic, Robin Lopez and Vlatko Cancar will participate at the event.

International basketball legends Dejan Bodiroga, Sasha Danilovic and Luis Scola will also be present.

Other former NBA players set to take part in the game include Dirk Nowitzki, Steve Nash, Leandro Barbosa, Vladimir Radmanovic, Sasha Pavlovic, Rasho Nesterovic, Beno Udrih, Primoz Brezec, Bostjan Nachbar, Zoran Dragic, Marko Simonovic and Marko Guduric.

The remaining participants are mostly current/former Slovenian players, as well as Miroslav Beric, who was an Olympic silver medalist, World Cup gold medalist and two-time EuroBasket champion with Yugoslavia.

The coaching staff includes former NBA coaches Alving Gentry, Kevin McHale and Igor Kokoskov, Miami Heat’s assistant coach Chris Quinn, and high profile European basketball head coaches such as Bozidar Maljkovic and Sasha Djordjevic, as well as Slovenian basketball legend Jure Zdovc, among others.

Chris Bosh will be in attendance at the event, while Jimmy Butler was expected to be on the lineup, but had commitments in China he had to tend to.

“The Night of the Dragon” will take place on August 24th, with the drafting of the players expected to be held the night before.

The Stozice Arena in Ljubljana will be filled to the last seat, with the tickets being sold out in just 20 minutes.
