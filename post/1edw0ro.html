Interesting FIBA fact to note for the Olympics: A player will be accredited with an assist if their pass led to made free throws

The free throw shooter only has to make at least 1 free throw for the assist to be rewarded. A pass leasing to an and one is only counted as one assist, only one assist can be rewarded for each play that results in points.

An interesting stat that is a significant statistical rule difference to the nba. I like the rule, a player should not be deprived of an assist stat if the recipient of the pass was fouled but then earns points by making the free throws.

Will be interesting to see if this inflates players per 36 assist averages in the Olympics compared to their normal assist averages in the nba.
