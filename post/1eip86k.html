Ruining Myths: Larry Bird was not unathletic until the back injury after the ‘85 season.

In fact he probably had above average athlecism for his size. He was the power forward until that back injury, Kevin McHale didn’t become the full time starter until ‘86. Concerning the athleticism of Larry Bird he was listed at 6’9 based on his draft card he got at age 18. He was probably closer to 6”11, look at his on court fight with Kareem who is listed at 7’2. There’s no way they’re a full 5” inches difference in their heights. So Larry was a legit big man, there should be no expectations of him running like a guard. But he could run the court with or without the ball a lot faster than most men his size. That’s not unathletic. Also he was as strong as a bull. But his best physical attributes was his quickness, not speed. He was lightning quick. Everybody bit on his fakes. Still the one and only player I’ve ever seen break ankles with just headfakes.
