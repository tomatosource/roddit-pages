Lebron for NBA Commissioner

After hearing WNBA Commissioner Cathy Engelbert completely fumble a softball question about racism in the WNBA fanbase, it got me thinking that it’s time that the NBA plan for Adam Silver’s replacement with someone representative of the league.

I nominate Lebron. He’s been the best ambassador of the sport over the last 20 years and has proven to be as adept in the business world as he is on the court. I have no doubt that he can handle the job.

The rumors have always been about him owning an NBA team and, while admirable, being NBA Commissioner would be Earth shattering. Maybe not as financially lucrative as team ownership, but just think about the things that he could do to improve the game and grow it even further.

The only downside is that we’d have to rename “The King” as “The Commish” which isn’t nearly as cool.
