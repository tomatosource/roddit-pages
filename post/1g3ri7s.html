No new NBA Almanac this season

Hello everyone, it is with great sadness I inform you that there will be no new NBA Almanac for this upcoming season. Due to life commitments which have taken precedence I have been unable to put the time and love into producing a NBA Almanac this season.

The NBA Almanac has always been a passion hobby of mine. Something which I would do because it would bring me peace and joy. This year that feeling slipped away as life took over. I always told myself that when that feeling slipped away it would mean I would have to take a step back from the Almanac. I cannot put out a product which I am proud of and that the fans can love without me putting in that same passion I have poured into my books over the last 10 years.

I hope this is a temporary pause on the Almanac and anticipate to be back next season. Thank you to everyone who has supported me over the last decade.

Niku
