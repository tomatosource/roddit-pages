A (Potentially) Missing Angle on The G.O.A.T. Debate

In relation to the ”Level of Competition” argument: why do we only talk about the top two players or so on a given roster - the Kareem/Magic Lakers; the Hakeem/Sampson Rockets; the Malone/Stockton Jazz, etc. when the depth down the bench matters significantly as well?

When an opposing starter in Jordan’s era got into foul trouble/needed a rest, how comparable were players 6-15 on a roster to the 6-15 now?

Seeing both Jordan’s and Lebron’s era of basketball (I’m in my early 40s), I feel like Lebron competed against better basketball players with regards to depth than Jordan, and that we’re leaving out an important part of the discussion when we fail to consider this aspect of competition.
