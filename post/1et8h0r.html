OKC’s current big 3 is older than the old OKC big 3 in 2012

As of today, SGA, Jalen Williams, and Chet are 26, 23, and 22 respectively. For comparison, KD, Westbrook, and Harden were 23, 23, and 22 in the 2012 finals. If we add up the current OKC big 3’s ages as of today’s date and compare it to the sum of the old big 3’s ages on June 21st 2012, we get 26197 days vs 25623 days.

Actually crazy how different the 2 teams are viewed. Today’s OKC is the second youngest team in the league and one of the contenders for the next dynasty with everybody saying they’ll probably run the league one day. Their future is seemingly bright after making the playoffs for the first time with SGA as the first option. Meanwhile, 2012 OKC had been to the CF the previous year and looked monstrous in the playoffs. They swept defending champs Mavs, beat Lakers in 6, beat Spurs who were on fire in 6, and were the favorites against the Miami superteam with 27 year old Lebron. The 2012 big 3 would go on to all join Team USA at the olympics that summer before the end of an era when James Harden got traded.
