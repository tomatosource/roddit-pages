Patrick Beverley is close to sign with Hapoel Tel Aviv

Following the first report on Sunday, Noa Poplinger (a very credible Israeli reporter) reports that Beverley is close to reach an agreement with the Tel Aviv club that plays in the Israeli Basketball Premier League and the Eurocup, and will try to help them win both titles.

Beverley will be the second NBA player to sign with Hapoel this summer, following Ish Wainright who joined the reds last week from the Phoenix Suns.
