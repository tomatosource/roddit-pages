Mitchell Wiggins was selected 23rd overall in the 1983 draft by the Indiana Pacers, but was then traded to the Bulls for Sidney Lowe and a 2nd round pick shortly after. He spent time on the Houston Rockets and Philadelphia 76ers before heading overseas to play in the Greek League here he was a 2x Greek League Scorer & 3x Greek League All-Star

Andrew Wiggins has missed a good amount of games during the 2022-23 NBA season due to his father’s health.

Shams reported on April 2023 that it was due to his father’s health - “Golden State’s Andrew Wiggins plans to attend Warriors-Thunder on Tuesday. The reason for Wiggins’ leave of absence since mid-February is that his father, Mitchell Wiggins, has been dealing with a serious medical situation, sources close to the situation say. “

Wiggins also missed some games during the 2023-24 season for “personal reasons” seemingly related to his father’s health again.

Wiggins had to cancel his shoe tour for Peak this summer due to a family emergency according to his manager Richard Clarke’s video on Weibo - “Unexpected things happen. Unforeseen circumstances that are out of control… We really appreciate the love and support that you guys provide for him… in due time we’ll come back and make it whole.”
