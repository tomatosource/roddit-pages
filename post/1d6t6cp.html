The Warriors without Klay Thompson in 2022 started the year 29-9(62 win Pace)

In the 28 games without James Wiseman in 2021 the Warriors played at a 60 win Pace!! Tracy Mcgrady has recently stated that Curry can’t make the playoffs with either Klay or Dray out so I just wanted to kind of call out how ridiculous this narrative is.

This is without even mentioning the Warriors being good playoff teams in 2013 and 2014 with Draymond still being a bench player and Curry being their lone star at the time(David Lee was an all star in 2013 but he missed the playoffs with an injury).
