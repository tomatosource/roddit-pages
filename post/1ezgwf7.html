Netflix teases a project following a group of basketball players, LeBron James, Shai Gilgeous-Alexander, Nikola Jokic, Victor Wembanyama, and Franz Wagner, during the 2024 Olympic tournament, to release in 2025

Source

Netflix teased the project with a post showing the jerseys of the five players laid out on the ground.(Note: The number of the German jersey is cut off, but I’m rather sure Wagner’s number 9 is the only number it could be) The post had text reading “Find out what happens on the quest for Olympic Gold. Coming to Netflix 2025.”.

Of course also the jerseys could not indicate what players will be featured, but I’m assuming they are.

​

The show would likely be in the vein of other sports documentaries on Netflix, such as Quarterback, Full Swing, Break Point, and Formula 1: Drive to Survive. Netflix also has an upcoming project featuring NBA players during the 2023-24 season, those players being James, Jayson Tatum, Jimmy Butler, Anthony Edwards and Domantas Sabonis.
