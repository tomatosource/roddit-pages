James Harden will be a guest at this year’s Adidas Eurocamp. Fellow NBA players Vasilije Micic, Nikola Jovic and Austin Rivers will be a part of the coaching staff at the camp.

Source

The Adidas Eurocamp is one of the most prestigious basketball camps in the world, meant for the top prospects to compete against each other in order to showcase their skills and try to impress NBA and Euroleague scouts.

The camp is held in Italy and is scheduled to start on June 1st and end on June 3rd, and includes prospects such as Nikola Djurisic (ranked 17th on the Ringer’s Mock Draft), Darryn Peterson (concensus top-5 2025 guard), Caleb Holt (concensus top-5 2026 guard), Jack Kayil (All-Tournament selection at the U18 Euroleague Finals) etc.

The camp’s coaching staff also includes notable NBA coaches like Dave Joerger, David Vanterpool, Chris Fleming, Rico Hines, Jason Terry and Jordan Ott.

Notable Eurocamp alumni includes players such as Jaylen Brown, Zion Williamson, Brandon Ingram, Darius Garland, Lu Dort, Goran Dragic, Bogdan Bogdanovic, Danilo Gallinari, Jusuf Nurkic etc.
