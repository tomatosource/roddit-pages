Wenyen Gabriel joins Maccabi Tel Aviv on a 1 year deal + an option.

Maccabi Tel-Aviv also play in the Euroleague (they use the Partizan’s court in Belgrade, Serbia as their “home” court in Euroleague games due to the on-going war - Partizan offered their service since October 8th, 2023).

The South Sudanese big man played for the Sacramento Kings, Portland Trail Blazers, New Orleans Pelicans, Brooklyn Nets, Los Angeles Clippers, and Los Angeles Lakers.

He will also take part in the upcoming Olympics in Paris.
