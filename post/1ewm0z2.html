2012-2013 Miami Heat

Crazy to look back on that Heat team which was well ahead of its time. All but four players on their roster were in the 6’8 - 6’10 height range. Which fits right in par with how the league is today.

Can’t find the video of David Fizdale speaking about it, but that’s just absurd to think of almost an entire NBA roster being right around 6’9 give or take outside of DWade, Mario Chalmers, Norris Cole & Ray Allen.

Along with having four players in LeBron James, James Jones, Mike Miller & Shane Battier than were more than capable up playing the guard spots.

Had to be times where Spo ran a lineup in the nature of:

PG: LeBron 6’9 250 SG: James Jones 6’8 218 SF: Mike Miller 6’8 218 PF: Shane Battier 6’8 220 C: Udonis Haslem 6’8 235

Crazy to think about.
