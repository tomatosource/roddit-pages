Going after offensive rebounds

There have been certain players (Josh Hart and Rajon Rondo come to mind) who have gotten their team many extra possessions by going hard after offensive rebounds, not necessary by crashing the boards but running into the paint area and picking off the rebounds that bounce a little further out. There are many teams though that have the players prioritize getting back on defense instead. It would seem be better to at least make some attempt to get the offensive rebound, the rest of the team can scramble back.
