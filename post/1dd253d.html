From the L2M description on the official.nba.com website:

Below is the league’s assessment of officiated events that occurred in the last two minutes of last night’s games that were at or within three points during any point in the last two-minutes of the fourth quarter (and overtime, where applicable)

was listening to the Lowe Post and heard Zach Lowe say “i cant wait for the Last 2 minute report”. realized it hadnt come out yet, and saw in the description that it’s only done for games within 3 pts during the last 2 minutes of the 4th.
