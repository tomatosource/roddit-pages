The Boston Celtics have now won 9 consecutive playoff games, becoming just the 10th team ever to do so in a single playoff run.

Dating back to Game 3 of the 2nd Round against the Cleveland Cavaliers, the Boston Celtics have now won 9 consecutive games this playoffs; 3 against the Cavaliers, 4 against the Pacers, and 2 (and counting) against the Mavericks.

They are now the 10th team in history to win 9 straight games in a single playoff run, joining the likes of:

  • 2017 Warriors (15 straight)
  • 1999 Spurs (12 straight)
  • 1989 Lakers (11 straight)
  • 2001 Lakers (11 straight)
  • 2003 Nets (10 straight)
  • 2012 Spurs (10 straight)
  • 2016 Cavaliers (10 straight)
  • 2017 Cavaliers (10 straight)
  • 2021 Suns (9 straight)
