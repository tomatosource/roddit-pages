The international game must adapt the US buzzer sound

The buzzer used in international games is comical. It sounds like there is a clown car just off screen with a clown at the controls, signaling the referees that a quarter is over or a substitute wants to go in.

The US buzzer sound is authoritative. It makes its presence felt instantly. There is no doubt about what you are hearing.

FIBA must adopt the US buzzer noise in order to make the international game truly competitive.
