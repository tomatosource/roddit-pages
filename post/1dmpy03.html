Breakdown For Me: Allen Iverson As The #1 Draft Prospect In 1996

Since we are in NBA draft season, I would love some old heads to educate me and everyone on how a barely 6-foot combo guard became the #1 draft in one of history’s most stacked NBA drafts.

I wasn’t old enough to watch like that, but I never understood how this happened, especially given how dominant the big guys were in the league then, with Shaq, Keem, Robinson, Mourning, etc.

Honestly, even if he was a prospect in today’s climate, I have a hard time believing any exec would want to draft an undersized combo guard, which is so crazy to me that he pulled it off in 1996.

Were his scoring and skillset so incomprehensible and unprecedented in basketball history that it was a sure thing they would translate over? That’s all I can pinpoint about what made a player like Iverson the #1 prospect in 1996.

I would love to learn more about this! Thank you!
