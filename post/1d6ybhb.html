Who’s the fourth best player of the 2010’s and is it Kawhi Leonard?

Clearly, the trio of LeBron, Curry and Durant own the top spots (whether you have Curry over KD or KD over Curry is another debate altogether), but similar to tennis there is a question of who is closest to the big three (Andy Murray maybe), and I think Kawhi has a decent argument.

Leonard’s accolades: 2 FMVPs, 2 championships, 1 DPOY, 6 all-NBA’s (1st teams in 2016, 2017 and 2021)

Some others that could contend for the no 4. spot: Westbrook, Harden, CP3 etc

This isn’t an attempt to create a Mount Rushmore, as I do think whoever slides in at no 4 is not nearly as relevant as the aforementioned three. However, Kawhi (barring Dirk and Kobe, but they were best in the 2000s) is the only number one option to win a championship during the trio’s primes, and he did it twice. While he doesn’t quite make a quartet with LBJ, Steph and KD, Leonard is in a separate trio as one of the three players to have won a FMVP and a DPOY, in the elite company of Michael Jordan and Hakeem Olajuwon.

Have I missed someone, or is the question itself obsolete?
