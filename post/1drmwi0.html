The Clippers failed and should be headed towards a rebuild

Think it’s very safe to say this era of the Los Angeles Clippers is a failure, with or without Paul George… his departure would only be acceleratibg the inevitable downfall. Their best three players are all past their prime, with Kawhi and Paul George both being injury prone to boot. These guys will never be healthy, and their supporting cast is mediocre and aging.

The Clippers aren’t true contenders and they don’t have any picks, so the future is looking bleak. The only way to salvage this disappointing era is to blow it up. Trade Harden and Kawhi for whatever they are worth at this point (doubt it’s much), trade the role players and maybe, just somehow you can regain at least half the picks you lost with the all time bad Paul George trade.

But who am I kidding, they just got their new arena, logo etc. Ballmer is too prideful to admit when he failed so we’ll see them suit up next season and pretending as if there isn’t a giant elephant in the room…
