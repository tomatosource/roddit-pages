Partizan Belgrade’s new signing Iffe Lundberg reveals that he turned down an offer from the Celtics in order to stay and have a bigger role in the Euroleague

Source

It wasn’t a difficult choice for me. It might sound a bit crazy, but I had no doubt at all. I would become something like a 15th man in the team. So, nothing with guaranteed playing time and opportunities. I’m not interested in that anymore. I need time on the court.

Lundberg is a 6’4” combo guard who played for Virtus Bologna the past season, where he averaged a 9/2/2 on 56% TS statline in the Euroleague and was the concensus clutchest player of the season, with multiple daggers and go-ahead shots.

The 29-year-old Dannish already had a short stint in the NBA in the past, playing only four games for the Suns during the 2021-22 season.

Lundberg will be playing for Serbian club Partizan Belgrade in the upcoming season, joining ex-NBA players Frank Ntilikina and Bruno Caboclo.
