Where does Steve Nash rank among the greatest shooters ever?

Steve Nash gets a bad rep within NBA discourse since many people believe both of his MVPs should’ve been given to another guy (even though I think neither of those seasons were actually his best year), but because of that I feel like he gets lost in the shuffle when talking about the all time greats. Obviously when it comes to shooting, we know the normal suspects that get brought up in that conversation. Steph, Klay, Ray, Reggie, Larry, etc. I rarely hear about Steve Nash though, and that’s surprising to me, so I’ve compiled a little list of numbers here.

From 2001-2012 (Nash’s prime years):

  • Percentages of 49.8⁄43.2⁄90.8

  • 10th in total made 3s

  • 4th in 3pt%

  • Hit 40+% of his 3s in 10 of those years, and 14 of his 18 seasons

  • Rim FG% on par with Tony Parker, albeit on far fewer attempts

  • All time FT% record until Steph broke it

  • 47.8% midrange shooter (on par with Dirk Nowitzki) on 4600 attempts, again far fewer

  • Most career 50-40-90 seasons with 4

Nash was just crazy efficient and he didn’t have any sort of cold zones on the court, it just seems like his “fake” MVPs and possibly his inferior shot attempt numbers hold him back from this conversation specifically. So, thoughts? Where does he stand amongst the league’s GOAT shooters?
