It’s been 24 years since a player won both, the scoring title and the championship in the same season

The last time was in the 1999⁄00 season, when Shaq averaged just under 30ppg, won the scoring title and went on to win the NBA title as well. Since 2000, only 3 scoring champions reached the Finals, Allen Iverson in 2001, Kevin Durant in 2012 and Steph Curry in 2016.

Lost in the First round (8): 2002 Iverson, 2003 T-Mac, 2005 Iverson, 2006 Kobe, 2007 Kobe, 2009 Wade, 2010 Durant, 2017 Westbrook,

Lost in the Second Round (6): 2008 LeBron, 2013 Melo, 2019 Harden, 2020 Harden, 2022 Embiid, 2023 Embiid

Lost in the Conference Finals (3): 2011 Durant, 2014 Durant, 2018 Harden

Didn’t make the Playoffs (3): 2004 T-Mac, 2015 Westbrook, 2021 Curry

It’s highly improbable for the Mavericks to come back and win the title, but if they do, it would be only the second time in the 21st century for a player to win both, the scoring title and NBA title.
