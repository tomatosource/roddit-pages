A little project that I made: All-Time NBA Teams, but players can’t repeat

Note: For some players like Shaq I decided to put them on a different team that they were less good on as long as 1) The team would be horrendous without them (imagine an Orlando all-time team without Shaq) and 2) They were still good on that team and considered one of its GOATs

https://i.imgur.com/l7CvaJx.png

Here are my conclusions:

Magic, Celtics, TWolves, Cavs, Hawks, Thunder, Bulls, Spurs, Jazz, Trailblazers, Suns, Pistons, Nuggets, Pacers, Grizzlies, Kings, Wizards, Pelicans, Knicks (19⁄30 teams) remain the same

Lakers, Rockets, Mavericks worsen, but only by a little

Bucks, Raptors, 76ers, Warriors have a noticeable change, but it’s not critical

Nets, Hornets, Heat, Clippers experience severe losses that really hurt the team
