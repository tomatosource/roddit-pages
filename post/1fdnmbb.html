True or False: Russell Westbrook has a comeback year for the Nuggets in the 2024⁄25 NBA season

Self-explanatory title and post. Do you all believe Russ has another…well, he won’t ever be MVP Russ, but can he have a level of effectiveness comparable to what we saw in the half season+playoffs with the Clippers in 2023? His shooting was outlier and very likely won’t be replicated, but I think the situation in Denver is an excellent one for him at this stage.

I think he gets a lot of time next to Jokic. The Nuggets are kinda gonna need him to hit. He’s gonna be their 6th man and be someone who’ll bring his classic slashing attacks, push the tempo in transition, and provide some of the attributes the Nuggets lacked a bit last season. A little more juice and pop as a team. I like it for him and for the Nuggets because though it’s not as prime levels, he’s still got the burst and athleticism to make an impact in the right role. I also think the Jokic+Russ PnR with either as the ballhandler could be something really fun and effective. Could also allow Murray to play a bit more off the ball, and he’s an excellent off ball shooter, something that hasn’t necessarily been utilized enough.

What do you all think? I don’t think it’s necessarily true or false….but I do see the vision and potential for success.
