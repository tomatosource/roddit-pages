The NBA added a 3rd All NBA team in 1989, when there were only 25 teams in the league. Once expansion happens, should the NBA add a 4th All NBA team?

Talent is at an all time high. And we will be at 32 teams. Not only that but individual roster sizes have also increased. All in all the talent pool is way bigger now. Is that enough to justify All NBA 4th team?

25 teams / 15 All NBA spots = 1.67 teams per spot

32 teams / 15 spots = 2.13 teams per spot

32 teams / 20 spots = 1.6 teams per spot

To maintain the balance that existed in 1989, a 4th All NBA team would be necessary
