Who was a better individual player in their prime: Victor Oladipo or Bradley Beal?

Bradley Beal in the ‘20-‘21 season:

— 31.3 PPG | 4.7 RPG | 4.4 APG

— 2nd leading NBA scorer

— All-NBA Third Team

— All-Star

Lead the Wizards to the playoffs (although without a winning record). Great shooter with better team success when he played a Robin role with John Wall.

Victor Oladipo in the ‘17-‘18 season:

— 23.1 PPG | 5.2 RPG | 4.3 APG

— 2.4 SPG (Led the League)

— All-NBA Third Team

— All-Star

— Most Improved Player

— All-Defensive First Team

Lead the Pacers to the 5th seed and a 7 game series against the Cavaliers. He was a good shooter and elite defender as well.
