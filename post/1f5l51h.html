Shooting percentages for 1st, 2nd and 3rd free throws

It seems players in the NBA in 2023-24 regular season hit 76.3% of their first attempts, 81.0% of their second attempts and 87.6% of their third attempts. The last one makes sense for the type of player who would likely get fouled while shooting a 3 pointer. The thing that intrigues me the most is that while 75.7% of 3PT plays (2PT + And-1) are completed, just 72.5% of 4PT plays are completed. This is based on my own program and I expect it to be about 99.9% accurate to the attempts and makes, but that last figure is a bit baffling. Other figures: 78.1% of free throws for 2PT shooting fouls, 85.8% for 3PT shooting fouls, and 80.6% for fouls from team penalty situations (personal fouls that generate free throws).
