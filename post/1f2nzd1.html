Who Had The Better Series?

As I was watching some old Luka highlights, particular his highlights vs the Warriors two years ago, I can’t help but feel like I have seen it before

The series Luka had vs the Warriors reminds me of the performance Steph put together in the 2019 Finals. Both players played terrific, both players played against historic defenses and both players didn’t have the help they deserved

Steph 2019 Finals: 30.5 points, 5.2 rebounds, 6 assists, 1.5 steals, 41.4% from the field, 34.3% from three, 94.7% from the line

Despite both players’ best efforts, it wasn’t enough as they lost in 5 and 6 respectively

Who do you think had the better series? This is a great question
