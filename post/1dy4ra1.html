Wemby and Gobert headline France’s final 12-man roster for the Paris Olympics

Source

This year’s ROY Victor Wembanyama, and the reigning and four-time DPOY Rudy Gobert are the main stars on France’s final roster for the Paris Olympics.

The remaining center for France will be this year’s All-Euroleague First Team selection Mathias Lessort, while the forward spots will be occupied by veteran NBA wing Nicolas Batum, last year’s Euroleague champion Guerschon Yabusele, the Wizards’ Bilal Coulibaly and former All-Eurocup First Team selection Isiah Cordinier.

The guard spots consist of former Pistons shooting guard Evan Fournier, the all-time top scorer in European continential competitions Nando De Colo, former number 8 overall draft pick Frank Ntilikina, Monaco’s Matthew Strazel and last year’s Eurocup champion Andrew Albicy.

France will be playing in the same group as Germany, Brazil and Japan at the Olympics.
