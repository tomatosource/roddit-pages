Giannis Antetokounmpo is the front-runner to be Greece’s flag bearer at the Paris Olympics

Source

Greece just qualified for the Paris Olympics by beating Croatia 80-69 in the final of the Olympic Qualifying Tournament in Piraeus.

This is the first time that they’ll play at the Olympics since 2008, and their group in Paris will also consist of Canada, Spain and Australia.

The other main candidate to be the flag bearer for Greece is likely Mitos Tentoglou, one of the best long jumpers in the world currently and one of the greatest long jumpers of all time.
