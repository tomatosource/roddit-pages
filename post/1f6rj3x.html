Teams scoring around 100 points per game was perfect and the NBA needs to get back to that.

90-100 points is plenty of action and every bucket still feels meaningful. Sub 90s can get a little ugly (ex 2004 pistons) so I definitely see why they decided to open the game up. Unfortunately, we’ve swung way too far in the other direction. 120ppg just makes every basket feel less meaningful and there are hardly any stops in a regular season game. Think about it, one of the elements of the 2016 game 7 that made it so memorable was that long stretch without any scoring in the fourth and how each shot felt like it had the weight of the universe on it. I think moving the 3pt line back and letting defenders be a bit more physical would ultimately move the league in the right direction.
