Question about the DPOY’s defense

Does he ever orchestrate his team to be in a position to bring guys into him? Guys like Draymond and Lebron, even if they are not in an ideal matchup, will adjust their teams entire defensive scheme on the fly so they can’t be exploited as easily.

Does Gobert when he gets put on an island constantly ever orchestrate a counter and put himself in a position to do what’s he’s good at? I always hear lauding about his defensive prowess as a paint protector, which is fair, but being able to audible as the backbone of the defense is equally important as soon as games get real (i.e. 7 games series where teams gameplan against you for 2 weeks).

I am not trying to talk shit, I just do not watch the Wolves or Jazz enough to know. It’s not necessarily his fault if he doesn’t do this but is worth praise if he does.
