But the question is about expansion draft. For every expansion, there is an expansion draft.

Current Rules:

Each Nba team have 15 players in their roster. And they can only protect 8 players. So, the 7 players for each team that is unprotected will be available for expansion draft.

So, the 2 expansion teams will select from those set of players. (7 x 30 (teams))= 210 unprotected players.
