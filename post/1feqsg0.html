The cultural legacy of Michael Jordan

As a middle aged man it’s interesting to me see the enduring cultural legacy of Michael Jordan. I will be driving by playgrounds or schools and see high school, middle school and even elementary school kids wearing the jersey and branded gear of a man whose career ended over twenty years ago. And whose best playing days were a nearly thirty years ago.

In contrast there was absolutely nothing like this when I was their age. I would not see other kids wearing Wilt Chamberlin jerseys or Oscar Robertson jerseys or Kareem Abdul-Jabaar jerseys.
