MIP Prediction For The Upcoming Season

As we look forward to next season, every year there are players who breakout. Time to predict who will win the MIP award, time to predict which players will take the leap

• Jalen Johnson: as the hawks go into rebuild mode, I see someone like a Johnson taking a huge leap. Coming off averaging 16 points a game this past season, I see Johnson taking another leap. I see him averaging 20 plus and firmly establishing himself as the second option next to Trae. He will also shoot a career high 55% from the field as having Trae will open up opportunities for him

• Paolo Banchero: some say that Paolo don’t belong on this list as he is already a star. With Orlando having a young core, they will continue to improve, same with Paolo. I see him taking a leap, becoming a superstar. I also see him averaging 26 to 27 points a game this season while being efficient

I was going to be put more players on this list but I feel like situations play a part. For Zion, I believe he will take the next step but it all depends on how severe his hamstring is and the situation with Ingram. I was going to put Mark Williams on here but he hasn’t been durable, nor do I see him being a big time scorer. He will play his role well, be a lob threat but don’t expect him to score a lot
